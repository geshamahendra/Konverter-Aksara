#Kuñjarakarna Dharmakathana#

ḍisalin oleh:geśa mahendra cunyadha

#Awighnamastu#

<Ragā kusuma>:1. 
– – – | ⏑ ⏑ – | ⏑ – ⏑| ⏑ ⏑ – | ⏑ ⏑ ⏑ | ⏑ ⏑ ⏑ | – ⏑ – | ⏑ ⏓

saṅ dhīrâmrih ayuddha riṅ raṇa tapabrata makalaga wīraṣaḍripu,
lagyabyūha samādhitattwaguṇa wāhananira karuṇādidhāraṇā,
sanmudrā dhwaja siṅhanāda japamantra waradhanuh acintyabhāwanā,
bodhijñāna śarottamâṅhilaṅakĕn ripu makaphala dharmaśūnyatā*

sākṣāt mūrti bhaṭāra śākyamuni mārawijaya sira saṅ samaṅkana,
maṅgĕh maṅgalaniṅ maṅö praṇata bhaktyaśikhara ri lĕbūni jöṅ nira,
stutyaṅkĕn pratimāpratiṣṭha ginĕlar makuṭawidhi winījan akṣara,
hinyaṅ ni ṅwaṅ amogha tan saha sake padanira mara janma ni ṅhulun*

lāwan deniṅ aṅarcanê pada bhaṭāra sugata pinakeṣṭidewatā,
nirwighnā ṅwaṅ amarna-marna mikĕtaṅ caritaniran adharmadeśanā,
aṅkĕn cāmaniyâmrakāśakĕna kastawanira pinawitra riṅ jagat,
paṅgil rakwa wĕnaṅ paṅahwata ri janmaniṅ ajar-ajar aṅrĕgĕp laṅö*

ndah sambodhanani ṅhulun ri sira saṅ kawi nipuṇa pinaṇḍitêṅ laṅö,
ndātan kojara talpakâṅidanidan ṅwaṅ aṅikĕta kathā palambaṅa,
tan saṅkeṅ wihikan tuhun maṅiriṅêṅ gati para kawi mātra riṅ laṅö,
tan samwas pwa kuraṅ paṅawruh apa tan kawi kumawi turuṅ wruh iṅ krama*

sotan ta ṅwaṅ apuṅguṅ atpada maṅö pati-tut i saṅ akīrti bhāṣita,
tonĕn lwir nya hirim hirim sama saroruha riṅ apa ya sādhya tuñjuṅa,
maṅkā teki hatur nya tan sasiriṅêṅ para kawi sapaniwyan iṅ lanö,
aṅhiṅ teki inantusâriwariwāṅikĕta carita de kawīśwara*

wwantĕn kuñjarakarna teki paṅaranya carita ya ta dadya pādika,
ndan wyarthênapus iṅ kadi ṅwaṅ aḍusun wiphala kadi mahâpupur harĕṅ,
simbantĕn makahaywaniṅ carita maṅkin amuwuhi panindaniṅ para,
wyaktinyâmuhareki hāsyakaraṇa ṅhiṅ apuran iki de mahājana*

śrī wairocana pūrwa niṅ carita kālanira pinarĕk iṅ watĕk sura,
ṅkānên bodhiwihāra nirmala samaṅkana ta siran adharmadeśanā,
akṣobhyādi sabodhisattwapariwāra marĕki sira bhaktyamūrṣita,
mwaṅ sakweh para bajrapāṇisuranātha yama baruṇa len dhanādhipa#

<Tiḍak ḍiketahui>:2
⏑ – – | ⏑ ⏑ – | ⏑ – – | ⏑ ⏑ ⏓

bharālipratisāra labdhê samaya,
sudharmādhikatattwa sampun katama,
wĕkasniṅ paramārthikôwus kahiḍĕp,
warah śrī ṣaḍabhijña kāruṇyamaya*

tĕlas rakwa bharālyamūjā prathama,
gumantyaṅ hyaṅ anukramâyajña marĕk,
sawaṅ wrĕṣṭyamrĕtaṅ sudharmaśrawaṇa,
awas lwir trĕṇa buddhiniṅ hyaṅ masawuṅ*

huwus pwêki dineśanan dharma kabeh,
tĕkap hyaṅ jina tuṣṭa harṣan rumĕṅö,
paḍâṅarcana sādare śrī sugata,
byatītan ry ulih iṅ watĕk hyaṅ sakala#

<Nawaharṣa>:3
⏑⏑– | ⏑⏑– | ⏑⏑⏑| ⏑⏑⏑| – ⏑⏑| – ⏑⏑| ⏓

atha kuñjarakarna carita matapêṅ waramerugiri,
pinakāgratapodhara tan alupa riṅ sugatasmaraṇa,
makasādhana yoganira saphalawīrya sinādhyanira,
tuwi rākṣasarūpa pinakamala hetuniran patapa*

hana teki ri lambuh iṅ acala gihāśrama pūrwamukha,
aṅuṅaṅ jaladhi prasama kadi tapowana riṅ gagana,
sĕh ikaṅ kusumâṅjrah aparimita sarwa phalanya-n atöb,
panĕḍĕṅnika tan pĕgat asira-siran kadi tan lĕwasa*

ri hujuṅ-hujuṅ iṅ paraṅ arĕja maṅuṅkul anoṅi juraṅ,
matĕḍun-tĕḍunan bañu kumusuh aghoṣa humuṅ gumuruh,
asurak lwir i cāmaranika magiraṅgiraṅ adrĕs aṅin,
anaṅis mara cātakanika hana mör bhramitêṅ gagana*

wruh agöṅ prihikaṅ giriwana kayu teja samâśryasinaṅ,
tĕkaniṅ kayu simbarĕn aniru wibhūṣyaṅĕne wwit ikā,
akaluṅkaluṅ odwad araras agĕlaṅgĕlaṅ arja kuniṅ,
hana wrĕkṣa kawuntat umarĕk irikaṅ walik-aḍĕp i sor#

<Śikhariṇī>:4.
⏑ – – | – – – | ⏑ ⏑ ⏑ | ⏑ ⏑ – | – ⏑ ⏑ | ⏑ ⏓

sawaṅ strīratna lwir niṅ acala lĕṅöṅ lĕṅlĕṅ alaṅö,
asiñjaṅ ñjrahniṅ pādapa huwus agandhāsĕkar arūm,
ghanāraṅ wastranyeki putih anawaṅ caṅlwi sahĕle,
sawitnyan her-talyābharaṇanika tejākara-kara*

rarasniṅ kambaṅ pahyas ikin awuwuh rūm hayu tinon,
baṅun līlā harṣan pasidĕha latā mĕṅgĕp aśila,
lĕnöṅ rūpanya dyah lalita maṅilo toya walahar,
huyaṅ ganya-ṅ rāt kāraṇaniṅ apĕpĕt patra kasirir*

hana lwir śrĕṅgāra-ṅ gagana lawan adrîḍĕpanika,
gĕrĕh mandrâlon paṅharih-arih i śabdanya karĕṅö,
kilat ramyâṅde raśmi paṅujiwatanyâmrih akire,
mĕluk mālap-sor waṅkawanika tĕkâñumbana bañu*

katon mātra jro pambĕkan i bañuniṅ parwatanadī,
arĕṅwâṅ-imba lwir halis iṅ anukĕr wāhu kawawa,
kapiṅsĕl wwahniṅ nyuh gaḍiṅ apiṅit emweṅ jala papah,
ri sahniṅ meghāpiṇḍa mawudawudâwrĕkṣa katilar*

alūm aṅlih tah pādapa lĕsu katīkṣṇan dinakara,
tuṣārâṅluh hisnya bhramara manaṅis mör kalĕmahan,
sawah rāhniṅ siñjaṅ drawa mara tahi-hyaṅnika mabāṅ,
tumuṅkultuṅkul mraknika mapa tikaṅ wastu dinĕlö#

<Turida gati>:5. 
⏑ ⏑ ⏑ | ⏑ – ⏑ | – ⏑ ⏑ | ⏑ – ⏑ | ⏑ ⏑ ⏑ | ⏑ ⏑ – | ⏑ – ⏑ | ⏓

tuhu mara yan wiśeṣagirirāja sakahawan aṅāśrayêṅ laṅö,
upaśama tiṅkah iṅ tahĕn ataṅkil apagĕh apa tan salah-bhawa,
praṇata maṅañjali lwir i sulur nya sahaja marĕk aṅharas lemah,
parĕṅ ahatur sĕkar bhujagapuṣpa surabhi sahaśoka campaka*

arĕṇa apawwahan gaḍiṅ ikaṅ tirisan amawa mañcuṅ aṅlugas,
jaladha ri agraniṅ gĕgĕr aṅuṅkul amisah asawaṅ payuṅ putih,
kahaḍaṅ asaṅgani swarani śaṅkhanika humuṅ i gĕṇḍiṅ iṅ manuk,
kadi hana bañwahoma titis iṅ juraṅ anĕkar-uraṅ sĕkar tibā*

wwara hawan iṅ wanāśrama tinuṇḍa pinarigi hinambalan watu,
sana-sini bañjaran sĕkar arūm sĕḍĕṅ amĕwĕh aṅambwakĕn laṅö,
cara-cara handwaṅ asla kayu mas kayu puriṅ acĕlĕkcĕlĕk putih,
parajita maśridanta jagaśatru pinaḍapaḍa len sĕkar kuniṅ*

paḍa tan asor sĕkar niṅ asanâsinaṅ akuniṅ apendah iṅ kuniṅ,
wijah apajöṅ kuniṅ kunĕṅ ikaṅ kusuma mekar atöb paḍâkuniṅ,
tĕka ni manuk nikâkuniṅ ikaṅ kapudaṅ anakĕbinya wah kuniṅ,
tuhu lakuniṅ hanêṅ gunuṅ awarna kasih-arĕp agañjaran kuniṅ*

patani pararyanan hana mahantĕn apatiga lĕyĕp kadîṅ tulis,
hatur ika sopacāra taru sarwaphala kusuma ramya len pucaṅ,
hujuṅan i patraniṅ pisaṅ-alas baṅun aharĕpaṅ āptyaneṅ mara,
bañu tan adoh ri sor titir aṅer-tali galita sakeṅ ruhur wukir*

kidaṅ adulur laki stryasĕmu polah iṅ atawatawan marêṅ wukir,
liriṅ i matanya lĕñjĕp amanis kadi maṅatag araryanêṅ priyā,
gaḍuṅ awilĕt silih-pĕkul aluṅ lwir atuduh amarantya riṅ bukur,
baṅun amĕḍar tĕṅah pupus ikaṅ liraṅ aputih anaṅhi kūṅ lulut*

apan ika rakwa mandaragiri prakaśita pinakādiniṅ wukir,
satata sabhā watĕk hyaṅ awilāsa dinulur ikaṅ apsarâpsarī,
karaṇa niṅ adri ramya racananya-n aniluman anopamêṅ laṅö,
abhinawa toya nirmala ri śrĕṅga hana raṇu patīrthan uttama*

apituwi śrĕṅganiṅ giri sumeru hibĕk ta atapa rṣyatūt gĕgĕr,
saḍukuḍukuh katon atĕp i raṅkaṅ ika pĕtuṅ anekawarnana,
hana taṅ inantya kāptin akĕtĕb-kĕtĕb anatap inuttamêṅ hajöṅ,
hana kumukus pilih ra tapa ganya sumĕlaṅ akulub-kulub gaṅan*

ika ta halĕpnya sor tĕkap iṅ āśrama patapaniraṅ mahāsura,
wiwara śilāputih kadi tutuknikaṅ acala sumeru kātara,
arata wiśāla marsik akilā sphaṭika wĕṅi jugā yayâpaḍaṅ,
bañu tumĕḍun sake ruhur aṅuṅkuli wiwara tibā marêṅ juraṅ*

yatiwara kuñjarāsura hanêṅ wiwara sĕḍĕṅ ayoga dhāraka,
aśila subaddha lagyaṅisapu-ṅ taṅan alih amitābhalakṣaṇa,
pinahabĕnĕr śarīranira niścala-n umulat iṅ agra nāsikā,
mari ta-n asattwadhātu kahiḍĕp wimala waluya jāti śūnyatā*

krama nira rākṣasa ndan iki lakṣaṇanira tuhu bhāwaniṅ wiku,
tĕhĕr akaluṅ gaṇitri magĕlaṅ guduha rahat iṅ ambarâtĕṅö,
paragi tĕṅö sinampĕtakĕn uttama satirun atĕṅwa riṅ manah,
kadi makuṭâjaṭâhalap ulah saṅ ataki-taki niṣparigraha*

ri huwus irâsamādhi sukhacitta larinira mijil sakeṅ giha,
bhramita wulat nirêṅ jaladhi ramya ri pasisi tĕkêṅ tĕṅah lĕyĕp,
wĕkasan awarna miśra lawan ambara lĕṅit ahirĕṅ katiṅhalan,
linawĕ-lawö nira-n kadi wĕkasniṅ ahurip ana nitya niṣphala*

kṣaṇa humĕnĕṅ sirâsĕmu wiraṅrwaṅ anuluyi manah panasbaran,
gumuṇita gupta riṅ twas awiweka satĕkapanikaṅ nayottama,
tan alawasaṅ prayojana tumĕmwa wibhawa tuwi marya rākṣasa,
karaṇa nirâkire marĕk anĕmbaha ri pada bhaṭāra gautama#

<Prĕthiwītala>:6.
⏑ – ⏑ | ⏑ ⏑ – | ⏑ – ⏑ | ⏑ ⏑ – | ⏑ – – | ⏑ ⏓

saṅ ugratapa śīghra maṅkat ahawan wiyat nirbhaya,
hana-n kadi khagendra maṅlayaṅ iṅ agraniṅ parwata,
swabhāwanira yakṣa śakti madulur prabhāwâdbhuta,
prahāra kumusuh tĕkâdrĕs amusus tahĕn riṅ wukir*

lĕpas larini râṅidul tucapa taṅ sakātuṅkulan,
pradeśa mapatuṅga-tuṅgalan akāṇḍa-kāṇḍâlaṅö,
tĕkêṅ wukir alasnya kubwan arĕṇĕb gagâtūt hiriṅ,
pucaṅ tirisan aryan arnĕb atatā tahĕn wwa-wwahan*

gĕgĕr paṅalusan hañar tinarukâpramāṇêṅ juraṅ,
huwus tinapak iṅ wanāgni sawalĕr-walĕr nyan gĕsĕṅ,
dudug tĕka riṅ agra mākrĕp atumaṅ harĕṅnyâpupul,
śilā kumalaśâputih rĕṅat arekha-rekhā pĕṭa*

ikaṅ gunuṅ aroṅ katon hana i sor giha lwir gĕḍoṅ,
paraṅ nika sumoṅ sajoṅ kadi masoṅ winoṅwoṅ mara,
sirôbwan umulat paḍâmawa wajoṅ kajoṅkoṅ tibā,
aboṅ umuni kagyat awri ginĕroṅ nikaṅ moṅ alon*

adödĕṅ aputih wwayiṅ walahar embuh akweh gunuṅ,
sawah sphaṭikamārga śuddha lumarap bañunyâlaris,
tutug tĕka ri windhyaparwata paḍâṅawetan lĕpas,
tumampuh i gihānikaṅ jaladhi ghūrnitâgĕnturan*

rĕrĕb sinawawêṅ riris kilat aliwran abhrâsinaṅ,
sateja dumilah prabhāwanira kuñjarâwyomaga,
paḍânah-anaha-n mulat kaburĕṅaṅ tumĕṅhêṅ laṅit,
mapêndah iki liṅ nikaṅ wwaṅ apa tan hanā maṅkana*

tiniṅhalan irâparö giri sumeru lambwaṅ kidul,
baṅun sira sinambhramêṅ alas akon marâraryana,
humuṅ swara nikaṅ manuk wija-wijah lwir aswāgata,
lutuṅ nika lumumpat agya susugun kadi kṣepa ya*

bhawiṣyati katon krĕtâbhinawa taṅ wihārâhalĕp,
agopura suwarna ratna mapucak maṇīndrārjuna,
ndatan pagati taṅ diwākara śaśāṅka keraṅ-iraṅ,
tĕkap niṅ atibhāswarânĕlĕhi bapra śuddhâkila*

wawaṅ sira tĕkêṅ wihāra warabodhicittâmala,
tumuntĕn umarĕk krĕtāñjali ri pāduka hyaṅ jina,
wruh iṅ paramadewakarṣaṇa wiśeṣapūjastuti,
ya kāraṇa bhaṭāra tuṣṭa tumuluymanantwê sira*

wĕkaṅku tija bhāgya saṅ tapa marĕk ri jöṅ ni ṅhulun,
ndya donta laki yan parêry aku mapaṅ pinintânaku,
huwus wruh aku yan tasak bratatapanta ṅūnîṅ wukir,
usön tĕpĕtakĕn juga prih ikanaṅ kayogīśwaran*

na liṅ nira bhaṭāra buddha sumahur ta saṅ tāpasa,
bhaṭāra huniṅan ṅhulun warahaniṅ sudharmādhika,
maran saphalajanma marya makajāti yakṣākrĕti,
apan pisaniṅun manĕmwakĕna wīrya dewaprabhu*

muwah mapa ta wastu sambulih i janmaniṅ rāt kabeh,
nimitta nika yan hana-ṅ tumĕmu duhkha lāwan sukha,
lĕwĕs mara ri tan paḍanya hana kasy-asih mwaṅ sugih,
pitowi hana pūrnarūpa sawaneh manaṇḍaṅ mala*

nahan pataña saṅ tapāsura ri jöṅ nira hyaṅ jina,
pradīpta rawitulya rakwa karuṇa-n bhaṭāreṅ jagat,
ya kāraṇani buddhi saṅ yati mekar sawaṅ paṅkaja,
halĕpnya kahatur marêṅ pada bhaṭāra tuṣṭâmuwus*

ya têku laki janma mūlya maṅaran pawitreṅ jagat,
ri denyan aharĕp ri dharma tuhu sādhu labdhêṅ hayu,
apan sipi kĕtaṅ hawas wihikaneṅ rusitniṅ dadi,
amĕṅpĕṅa ri kālaniṅ hurip aṅiṣṭya dharmôttama*

hana wruh i kadibyan iṅ paramadharma yan gĕgwana,
tathāpi tiḍapuccha tan harĕp atakwanêṅ paṇḍita,
pijĕr jĕnĕk amukti wīrya kasukhan ginönnyêniwö,
mataṅnya larapanya wismrĕti jaḍêṅ sudharmakrama*

swabhāwanikanaṅ lalĕr maharĕp iṅ kudis rāh kanin,
purīṣa mahaha śrĕgāla bawi gāgak āptîṅ dagiṅ,
tukar wyasana kāptiniṅ wwaṅ atimūḍha mūrkhâwĕrö,
sudharma hita kahyun iṅ paramasādhu tan gĕṅ galak*

hana wwaṅ awamāna liṅnya taya dharmaniṅ dewatā,
anāgatanikaṅ gawe hala-hayu ndya donyan tĕmu,
nihan sanu sadrĕbyaniṅ hyaṅ alapĕnku norânulah,
pitowin ika drĕwyaniṅ wwaṅ aparan kawĕdyêriya*

ikaṅ wwaṅ awamāna maṅkana pĕgat hiḍĕpnyeṅ hati,
ndatan tuhu pĕgat kasaṅśayan ikâwĕrö kewala,
lĕhĕṅ gati nikaṅ tahĕn sapadi janma mattâdhama,
apan dadi lĕpas pratiṣṭhanĕn i mantra saṅ paṇḍita#

<Śārdūlawikrīḍita>:7. 
– – – | ⏑ ⏑ – | ⏑ – ⏑ | ⏑ ⏑ – | – – ⏑| – – ⏑| ⏓

āpan durlabha taṅ dadi wwaṅ inusir niṅ sarwabhāwêṅ jagat,
yadyan mānuṣa durlabha-ṅ wwaṅ atĕmah jalwâmiśeṣêṅ priyā,
yan jalwâtika durlabha-ṅ wwaṅ atĕmah widwān suśīlâpagĕh,
yadyan paṇḍita durlabhā wihikanêṅ widyākriyādyāgama*

yadyan wruh-wruha riṅ kriyāgama tuhun saṅ wruh riṅ arṣârusit,
yadyan wruh-wruha rakwa mewĕh ikaṅ utsāhêṅ samastāgama,
saṅ dhīrêṅ sakalāgamârusit ikaṅ nityâmarah riṅ jagat,
yadyan mājara riṅ jagat saṅ aharĕp riṅ dharma mewĕh dahat*

putraṅku pwa pawitradharma kaharĕptāṅhiṅ wĕkaskun hiḍĕp,
konkonĕn rumuhun kitānaku lakun prāpteṅ kawah hyaṅ yama,
donantan wulateṅ samastagatisaṅsāreṅ kawah pātaka,
salwir nyan tañakĕn ri saṅ yamapati ry antukta tākwājara*

lwāmbĕktānaku haywa saṅśaya mĕne waswas jugaṅ pātaka,
rapwan teki tĕpĕt kite sarasaniṅ dharmāpagĕh niścaya,
āpan byakta panonta duṣkrĕtinikaṅ sañjīwasattwādhama,
śīghrātānaku maṅkatāstu kita nirwighnan kuminkin hayu*

ṅkā saṅ kuñjarakarna maṅkat i huwusnyāmwit ri saṅ hyaṅ jina,
sotan rākṣasa siddhacāraṇa sumīlĕm riṅ samudrālaris,
prāpteṅ bāyucatuṣpathe tĕṅah ikaṅ pat mārga maṇḍĕg laku,
wwantĕn bhūta si kālagupta kadunuṅ kālih lawan niṣkala*

yekānantwa ri kuñjarāsura paran dontan para ṅke laki,
lawan syāpa ṅaranta toh warahakĕn wāk jātya haywākalib,
āpan ṅwaṅ rwa rumakṣa mārga ḍatĕṅ iṅ swargā lawan rorawa,
akwīkin matuduh swakarmanikaṅ ātmānuṅ paranyan dunuṅ*

mojar saṅ tapa tan dwa mājar irikaṅ bhūtāṅĕmit riṅ hawan,
ṅwaṅ saṅ kuñjarakarna teki paṅarankwiṅ rāt mahārākṣasa,
saṅ hyaṅ śīghran asanmateṅ hulun akon prāpteṅ bhaṭārāntaka,
nāhan donku ḍatĕṅ tuhun warahakĕn taṅ mārga maṅken tutĕn*

lawan teki warah ṅhulun ri paran iṅ mārgāñaturdikwidik,
ndyāṅ ātmā maliwat hawan mapa tika lwir nyan padudwan hĕnū,
ah maṅkā pwa tañanta mājarakĕna ṅwaṅ yan kitāhyun wruha,
ai saṅ kuñjarakarna rĕṅwakĕna tah śabdaṅku yatnā kita*

riṅ pūrweśwaramārga yeka hawan iṅ śaktīṅ tapa mwaṅ brata,
nā lor iṅ harilokamārga hawan iṅ śūrātapeṅ papraṅan,

donkwātĕmwa lawan kitāpagĕh inuṅsir apatutan asānak iṅ ṭikā*

riṅ janmāntara tan saheṅ kita yadin satĕmahani ariṅku riṅ taman,
yadyan tuñjuṅa masku dadya talagaṅ hulun ahĕniṅ anindya paṅdyusan,
donya ṅwaṅ waraniṅ rarā yan adamū ṅhiṅ atĕwasa pawitra don ikā,
yan kālanta sināhaseṅalap amogha madalĕma kakanta rakṣakā*

yan paṅdadya kitāsana ṅwaṅ ibu dadya jalada marĕk aṅhĕmū jawuh,
bhuktin sihkwatĕmah riris yak agĕrĕh mapaga sĕmini kuṅta hat-hatĕn,
ndak ton harṣani maskwakĕmbaṅ anĕḍĕṅ wruha kita ri wilāśani ṅhulun,
ndan riṅ ṣatpada tan saheṅ marĕk i rūmta katuturana deṅkwañumbana*

nāhan lambaṅ i saṅ wiśāta winacanya winaluwaluyan wināhyakĕn,
ambĕkniṅ wiwudhāṅganālara tĕñuh kadi kahilaṅan ātmajīwita,
maṅkin tan kawĕnaṅ kinas laranika lwir apuyamaṅaniṅ mraṅ ujwala,
sambatnyāmlas-asih titir musapi luhnika malupa ri luṅsuriṅ tapih*

ah saṅ duṣṭa kiteṅ paturwan apa yan tan awĕdi ri warāstraniṅ smara,
ṅkā pamraṅniṅ alisniṅ adyah aluṅid kĕlab i pamatan iṅ kinewala,
tan kewĕh ri panumbak iṅ susu jajanta kaka pujipuji sthiraṅ brata,
siṅ heman kararaṅku mūlya hinalap tatan awĕlas añidra ri ṅhulun*

luṅhātiṅgal adoh kita ṅwaṅiṅanuṅgalakĕna paṅucapta māsihan,
enak ṅūni sinākṣyan iṅ madana bhāgya ri hati pĕjaheṅ pasaṅgaman,
wāhyāsatya tĕkapta mojar anĕsĕp laṭi tĕwas atalĕh luhik dahat,
sotkwaṅhiṅ sira widhyaniṅ smara panumpala wĕnaṅa maluymarêṅ hulun*

lwāmbĕk māsku mĕneṅ kulĕm mĕkasana ṅwaṅ asĕkuṅa sudhīra maṅhyaṅa,
pūjan riṅ śayanāsĕkar paṅisiniṅ gĕluṅ asĕpana gandhaniṅ pupur,
lumrāṅ kĕmbaṅ urā sugandha burat amrik amĕḍara tapihnya lepana,
rūpākāra gĕgön tĕlas haturanaṅkwi kita caru tĕṅah palar tĕka*

maṅkā sambat iṅ apsarī katanĕhan lara turida tiniṅgal iṅ sĕnĕṅ,
lwir norāpsaranātha sambhawa siwin gatinika pada lĕṅlĕṅ iṅ manah,
tar wruh paṅdan ikaṅ widhī hana riṅ āśrama phalanika puṇya riṅ jagat,
byakta drĕwyani saṅ miwöṅ kuśaladharmaṅ amarapada len warāpsarī*

nāhan hīṅanikiṅ kathe huwus i pūrnawijaya gumawe tapabrata,
rikāneṅ hyaṅ wara meruparwata lawan kasihirâpagĕh mahāyani,
sampun kuñjarakarna siddha lĕpas iṅ jinapada tumĕmuṅ kamokṣakan,
ṅkā rakweki muwah lĕpas prawara pūrnawijaya sira mokṣa sapriyā*

yêkī kuñjarakarna dharmakathana kṣamakĕna kĕta de saṅ aṅrĕṅö,
tan samwas katunan laṅö tĕkapira mpu ḍusun awĕtu mātrabhāṣita,
puṣpāñjalya ri jöṅ niran kawi samenaka pakĕnanirāstu sanmatan,
tĕkwan sihnira antusān panikĕlan tanah amuruki tiṅkah iṅ maṅö*

wyarthaṅ nāṭyan amenak iṅ śruti tuhun palalun iki paṅantyan-antyanan,
yadyan kālihanāpusĕn muwah apaṅlwaṅ anĕmu kawi rowaṅ iṅ maṅö,
ndonyā tan kawurāpus i ṅhulun aṅuṅsya paramapadanāgawe hayu,
