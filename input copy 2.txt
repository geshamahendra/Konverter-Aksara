*Om̃ śrī paramātmanè namaḥ*
*atha gītā dhyāna ślokāḥ*

Om̃ pārthāya pratibodhitāṃ bhagavatā nārāyaṇèna svayaṃ vyāsèna grathitāṃ purāṇamuninā madhyè mahābhāratam,
advaitāmṛtavarṣiṇīṃ bhagavatīṃ aṣṭādaśādhyāyinīṃ amba tvāṃ anusandadhāmi bhagavadgītè bhavadvèṣiṇīm*

namo'stutè vyāsa viśālabuddhè phullāravindāyatapatranètra,
yèna tvayā bhārata tailapūrṇaḥ prajvālito jñānamayaḥ pradīpaḥ*

prapannapārijātāya totravètraikapāṇayè,
jñānamudrāya kṛṣṇāya gītāmṛtaduhè namaḥ*

vasudèvasutaṃ dèvaṃ kaṃsachāṇūramardanam,
dèvakīparamānandaṃ kṛṣṇaṃ vandè jagadgurum*

bhīṣmadroṇataṭā jayadrathajalā gāndhāranīlotpalā śalyagrāhavatī kṛpèṇa vahanī karṇèna vèlākulā,
aśvatthāmavikarṇaghoramakarā duryodhanāvartinī sottīrṇā khalu pāṇḍavai raṇanadī kaivartakaḥ kèśavaḥ*

pārāśaryavachaḥ sarojamamalaṃ gītārthagandhotkaṭaṃ nānākhyānakakèsaraṃ harikathā sambodhanābodhitam,
lokè sajjanaṣaṭpadairaharahaḥ pèpīyamānaṃ mudā bhūyādbhāratapaṅkajaṃ kalimala pradhvaṃsinaḥ śrèyasè*

mūkaṃ karoti vāchālaṃ paṅguṃ laṅghayatè girim,
yatkṛpā tamahaṃ vandè paramānandamādhavam*

śāntākāraṃ bhujagaśayanaṃ padmanābhaṃ surèśaṃ viśvādhāraṃ gaganasadṛśaṃ mèghavarṇaṃ śubhāṅgam,
lakṣmīkāntaṃ kamalanayanaṃ yogihṛddhyānagamyaṃ vandè viṣṇuṃ bhavabhayaharaṃ sarva lokaikanātham*

yaṃ brahmāvaruṇèndrarudramarutaḥ stunvanti divyaiḥ stavaiḥ vèdaiḥ sāṅgapadakramopaniṣadaiḥ gāyanti yaṃ sāmagāḥ,
dhyānāvasthita tadgatèna manasā paśyanti yaṃ yoginaḥ yasyāntaṃ na vidussurāsuragaṇāḥ dèvāya tasmai namaḥ*

nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya narañchaiva narottamam,
dèvīṃ sarasvatīṃ vyāsaṃ tato jayamudīrayèt*

sachchidānandarūpāya kṛṣṇāyākliṣṭakāriṇè,
namo vèdāntavèdyāya guravè buddhisākṣiṇè*

sarvopaniṣado gāvaḥ dogdhā gopālanandanaḥ,
pārtho vatsaḥ sudhīrbhoktā dugdhaṃ gītāmṛtaṃ mahat*

gītāśāstramidaṃ puṇyaṃ yaḥ paṭhèt prayataḥ pumān,
viṣṇoḥ padamavāpnoti bhayaśokādi varjitaḥ*

èkaṃ śāstraṃ dèvakīputragītaṃ èko dèvo dèvakīputra èva,
èko mantrastasya nāmāni yāni karmāpyèkaṃ tasya dèvasya sèvā*

:1:*Arjunaviṣādayogaḥ*

*atha prathamo'dhyāyaḥ, arjunaviṣādayogaḥ*

dhṛtarāṣṭra uvāca,

:1\1\1:dharmakṣetre kurukṣetre samavetā yuyutsavaḥ,
māmakāḥ pāṇḍavāścaiva kimakurvata sañjaya*

sañjaya uvāca,

:2\1\2:dṛṣṭvā tu pāṇḍavānīkaṃ vyūḍhaṃ duryodhanastadā,
ācāryamupasaṅgamya rājā vacanamabravīt*

:3\1\3:paśyaitāṃ pāṇḍuputrāṇāmācārya mahatīṃ camūm,
vyūḍhāṃ drupadaputreṇa tava śiṣyeṇa dhīmatā*

:4\1\4:atra śūrā maheṣvāsā bhīmārjunasamā yudhi,
yuyudhāno virāṭaśca drupadaśca mahārathaḥ*

:5\1\5:dhṛṣṭaketuścekitānaḥ kāśirājaśca vīryavān,
purujitkuntibhojaśca śaibyaśca narapuṅgavaḥ*

:6\1\6:yudhāmanyuśca vikrānta uttamaujāśca vīryavān,
saubhadro draupadeyāśca sarva eva mahārathāḥ*

:7\1\7:asmākaṃ tu viśiṣṭā ye tānnibodha dvijottama,
nāyakā mama sainyasya saṃjñārthaṃ tānbravīmi te*

:8\1\8:bhavānbhīṣmaśca karṇaśca kṛpaśca samitiñjayaḥ,
aśvatthāmā vikarṇaśca saumadattistathaiva ca*

:9\1\9:anye ca bahavaḥ śūrā madarthe tyaktajīvitāḥ,
nānāśastrapraharaṇāḥ sarve yuddhaviśāradāḥ*

:10\1\10:aparyāptaṃ tadasmākaṃ balaṃ bhīṣmābhirakṣitam,
paryāptaṃ tvidameteṣāṃ balaṃ bhīmābhirakṣitam*

:11\1\11:ayaneṣu ca sarveṣu yathābhāgamavasthitāḥ,
bhīṣmamevābhirakṣantu bhavantaḥ sarva eva hi*

:12\1\12:tasya sañjanayanharṣaṃ kuruvṛddhaḥ pitāmahaḥ,
siṃhanādaṃ vinadyoccaiḥ śaṅkhaṃ dadhmau pratāpavān*

:13\1\13:tataḥ śaṅkhāśca bheryaśca paṇavānakagomukhāḥ,
sahasaivābhyahanyanta sa śabdastumulo'bhavat*

:14\1\14:tataḥ śvetairhayairyukte mahati syandane sthitau,
mādhavaḥ pāṇḍavaścaiva divyau śaṅkhau pradadhmatuḥ*

:15\1\15:pāñcajanyaṃ hṛṣīkeśo devadattaṃ dhanañjayaḥ,
pauṇḍraṃ dadhmau mahāśaṅkhaṃ bhīmakarmā vṛkodaraḥ*

:16\1\16:anantavijayaṃ rājā kuntīputro yudhiṣṭhiraḥ,
nakulaḥ sahadevaśca sughoṣamaṇipuṣpakau*

:17\1\17:kāśyaśca parameṣvāsaḥ śikhaṇḍī ca mahārathaḥ,
dhṛṣṭadyumno virāṭaśca sātyakiścāparājitaḥ*

:18\1\18:drupado draupadeyāśca sarvaśaḥ pṛthivīpate,
saubhadraśca mahābāhuḥ śaṅkhāndadhmuḥ pṛthakpṛthak*

:19\1\19:sa ghoṣo dhārtarāṣṭrāṇāṃ hṛdayāni vyadārayat,
nabhaśca pṛthivīṃ caiva tumulo'bhyanunādayan* 

:20\1\20:atha vyavasthitāndṛṣṭvā dhārtarāṣṭrān kapidhvajaḥ,
pravṛtte śastrasampāte dhanurudyamya pāṇḍavaḥ*

hṛṣīkeśaṃ tadā vākyamidamāha mahīpate,

arjuna uvāca,

senayorubhayormadhye rathaṃ sthāpaya me'cyuta :21\1\21:*

:22\1\22:yāvadetānnirīkṣe'haṃ yoddhukāmānavasthitān,
kairmayā saha yoddhavyamasmin raṇasamudyame*

:23\1\23:yotsyamānānavekṣe'haṃ ya ete'tra samāgatāḥ,
dhārtarāṣṭrasya durbuddheryuddhe priyacikīrṣavaḥ*

sañjaya uvāca,

:24\1\24:evamukto hṛṣīkeśo guḍākeśena bhārata,
senayorubhayormadhye sthāpayitvā rathottamam*

:25\1\25:bhīṣmadroṇapramukhataḥ sarveṣāṃ ca mahīkṣitām,
uvāca pārtha paśyaitānsamavetānkurūniti*

:26\1\26:tatrāpaśyatsthitānpārthaḥ pitṝnatha pitāmahān,
ācāryānmātulānbhrātṝnputrānpautrānsakhīṃstathā*

:27\1\27:śvaśurānsuhṛdaścaiva senayorubhayorapi,
tānsamīkṣya sa kaunteyaḥ sarvānbandhūnavasthitān*

kṛpayā parayāviṣṭo viṣīdannidamabravīt,

arjuna uvāca,

dṛṣṭvemaṃ svajanaṃ kṛṣṇa yuyutsuṃ samupasthitam :28\1\28:*

:29\1\29:sīdanti mama gātrāṇi mukhaṃ ca pariśuṣyati,
vepathuśca śarīre me romaharṣaśca jāyate*

:30\1\30:gāṇḍīvaṃ sraṃsate hastāttvakcaiva paridahyate,
na ca śaknomyavasthātuṃ bhramatīva ca me manaḥ*

:31\1\31:nimittāni ca paśyāmi viparītāni keśava,
na ca śreyo'nupaśyāmi hatvā svajanamāhave*

:32\1\32:na kāṅkṣe vijayaṃ kṛṣṇa na ca rājyaṃ sukhāni ca,
kiṃ no rājyena govinda kiṃ bhogairjīvitena vā*

:33\1\33:yeṣāmarthe kāṅkṣitaṃ no rājyaṃ bhogāḥ sukhāni ca,
ta ime'vasthitā yuddhe prāṇāṃstyaktvā dhanāni ca*

:34\1\34:ācāryāḥ pitaraḥ putrāstathaiva ca pitāmahāḥ,
mātulāḥ śvaśurāḥ pautrāḥ śyālāḥ sambandhinastathā*

:35\1\35:etānna hantumicchāmi ghnato'pi madhusūdana,
api trailokyarājyasya hetoḥ kiṃ nu mahīkṛte*

:36\1\36:nihatya dhārtarāṣṭrānnaḥ kā prītiḥ syājjanārdana,
pāpamevāśrayedasmānhatvaitānātatāyinaḥ*

:37\1\37:tasmānnārhā vayaṃ hantuṃ dhārtarāṣṭrānsvabāndhavān,
svajanaṃ hi kathaṃ hatvā sukhinaḥ syāma mādhava*

:38\1\38:yadyapyete na paśyanti lobhopahatacetasaḥ,
kulakṣayakṛtaṃ doṣaṃ mitradrohe ca pātakam*

:39\1\39:kathaṃ na jñeyamasmābhiḥ pāpādasmānnivartitum,
kulakṣayakṛtaṃ doṣaṃ prapaśyadbhirjanārdana*

:40\1\40:kulakṣaye praṇaśyanti kuladharmāḥ sanātanāḥ,
dharme naṣṭe kulaṃ kṛtsnamadharmo'bhibhavatyuta*

:41\1\41:adharmābhibhavātkṛṣṇa praduṣyanti kulastriyaḥ,
strīṣu duṣṭāsu vārṣṇeya jāyate varṇasaṅkaraḥ*

:42\1\42:saṅkaro narakāyaiva kulaghnānāṃ kulasya ca,
patanti pitaro hyeṣāṃ luptapiṇḍodakakriyāḥ*

:43\1\43:doṣairetaiḥ kulaghnānāṃ varṇasaṅkarakārakaiḥ,
utsādyante jātidharmāḥ kuladharmāśca śāśvatāḥ*

:44\1\44:utsannakuladharmāṇāṃ manuṣyāṇāṃ janārdana,
narake niyataṃ vāso bhavatītyanuśuśruma*

:45\1\45:aho bata mahatpāpaṃ kartuṃ vyavasitā vayam,
yadrājyasukhalobhena hantuṃ svajanamudyatāḥ*

:46\1\46:yadi māmapratīkāramaśastraṃ śastrapāṇayaḥ,
dhārtarāṣṭrā raṇe hanyustanme kṣemataraṃ bhavet*

sañjaya uvāca,

:47\1\47:evamuktvārjunaḥ saṅkhye rathopastha upāviśat,
visṛjya saśaraṃ cāpaṃ śokasaṃvignamānasaḥ*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde, 
arjunaviṣādayogo nāma prathamo'dhyāyaḥ*0*:1:*0*

:2:*Sāṅkhyayogaḥ*

atha dvitīyo'dhyāyaḥ, sāṅkhyayogaḥ

sañjaya uvāca,

:48\2\1:taṃ tathā kṛpayāviṣṭamaśrupūrṇākulekṣaṇam,
viṣīdantamidaṃ vākyamuvāca madhusūdanaḥ*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:49\2\2:kutastvā kaśmalamidaṃ viṣame samupasthitam,
anāryajuṣṭamasvargyamakīrtikaramarjuna*

:50\2\3:klaibyaṃ mā sma gamaḥ pārtha naitattvayyupapadyate,
kṣudraṃ hṛdayadaurbalyaṃ tyaktvottiṣṭha parantapa*

arjuna uvāca,

:51\2\4:kathaṃ bhīṣmamahaṃ saṅkhye droṇaṃ ca madhusūdana,
iṣubhiḥ pratiyotsyāmi pūjārhāvarisūdana*

:52\2\5:gurūnahatvā hi mahānubhāvān śreyo bhoktuṃ bhaikṣyamapīha loke,
hatvārthakāmāṃstu gurūnihaiva bhuñjīya bhogān rudhirapradigdhān*

:53\2\6:na caitadvidmaḥ kataranno garīyo yadvā jayema yadi vā no jayeyuḥ,
yāneva hatvā na jijīviṣāmas te'vasthitāḥ pramukhe dhārtarāṣṭrāḥ*

:54\2\7:kārpaṇyadoṣopahatasvabhāvaḥ pṛcchāmi tvāṃ dharmasammūḍhacetāḥ,
yacchreyaḥ syānniścitaṃ brūhi tanme śiṣyaste'haṃ śādhi māṃ tvāṃ prapannam*

:55\2\8:na hi prapaśyāmi mamāpanudyād yacchokamucchoṣaṇamindriyāṇām,
avāpya bhūmāvasapatnamṛddhaṃ rājyaṃ surāṇāmapi cādhipatyam*

sañjaya uvāca,

:56\2\9:evamuktvā hṛṣīkeśaṃ guḍākeśaḥ parantapa,
na yotsya iti govindamuktvā tūṣṇīṃ babhūva ha*

:57\2\10:tamuvāca hṛṣīkeśaḥ prahasanniva bhārata,
senayorubhayormadhye viṣīdantamidaṃ vacaḥ*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:58\2\11:aśocyānanvaśocastvaṃ prajñāvādāṃśca bhāṣase,
gatāsūnagatāsūṃśca nānuśocanti paṇḍitāḥ*

:59\2\12:na tvevāhaṃ jātu nāsaṃ na tvaṃ neme janādhipāḥ,
na caiva na bhaviṣyāmaḥ sarve vayamataḥ param*

:60\2\13:dehino'sminyathā dehe kaumāraṃ yauvanaṃ jarā,
tathā dehāntaraprāptirdhīrastatra na muhyati*

:61\2\14:mātrāsparśāstu kaunteya śītoṣṇasukhaduḥkhadāḥ,
āgamāpāyino'nityāstāṃstitikṣasva bhārata*

:62\2\15:yaṃ hi na vyathayantyete puruṣaṃ puruṣarṣabha,
samaduḥkhasukhaṃ dhīraṃ so'mṛtatvāya kalpate*

:63\2\16:nāsato vidyate bhāvo nābhāvo vidyate sataḥ,
ubhayorapi dṛṣṭo'ntastvanayostattvadarśibhiḥ*

:64\2\17:avināśi tu tadviddhi yena sarvamidaṃ tatam,
vināśamavyayasyāsya na kaścitkartumarhati*

:65\2\18:antavanta ime dehā nityasyoktāḥ śarīriṇaḥ,
anāśino'prameyasya tasmādyudhyasva bhārata*

:66\2\19:ya enaṃ vetti hantāraṃ yaścainaṃ manyate hatam,
ubhau tau na vijānīto nāyaṃ hanti na hanyate*

:67\2\20:na jāyate mriyate vā kadācin nāyaṃ bhūtvā bhavitā vā na bhūyaḥ,
ajo nityaḥ śāśvato'yaṃ purāṇo na hanyate hanyamāne śarīre*

:68\2\21:vedāvināśinaṃ nityaṃ ya enamajamavyayam,
kathaṃ sa puruṣaḥ pārtha kaṃ ghātayati hanti kam*

:69\2\22:vāsāṃsi jīrṇāni yathā vihāya navāni gṛhṇāti naro'parāṇi,
tathā śarīrāṇi vihāya jīrṇā nyanyāni saṃyāti navāni dehī*

:70\2\23:nainaṃ chindanti śastrāṇi nainaṃ dahati pāvakaḥ,
na cainaṃ kledayantyāpo na śoṣayati mārutaḥ*

:71\2\24:acchedyo'yamadāhyo'yamakledyo'śoṣya eva ca,
nityaḥ sarvagataḥ sthāṇuracalo'yaṃ sanātanaḥ*

:72\2\25:avyakto'yamacintyo'yamavikāryo'yamucyate,
tasmādevaṃ viditvainaṃ nānuśocitumarhasi*

:73\2\26:atha cainaṃ nityajātaṃ nityaṃ vā manyase mṛtam,
tathāpi tvaṃ mahābāho naivaṃ śocitumarhasi*

:74\2\27:jātasya hi dhruvo mṛtyurdhruvaṃ janma mṛtasya ca,
tasmādaparihārye'rthe na tvaṃ śocitumarhasi*

:75\2\28:avyaktādīni bhūtāni vyaktamadhyāni bhārata,
avyaktanidhanānyeva tatra kā paridevanā*

:76\2\29:āścaryavatpaśyati kaścidenamāścaryavadvadati tathaiva cānyaḥ,
āścaryavaccainamanyaḥ śṛṇotiśrutvāpyenaṃ veda na caiva kaścit*

:77\2\30:dehī nityamavadhyo'yaṃ dehe sarvasya bhārata,
tasmātsarvāṇi bhūtāni na tvaṃ śocitumarhasi*

:78\2\31:svadharmamapi cāvekṣya na vikampitumarhasi,
dharmyāddhi yuddhācchreyo'nyatkṣatriyasya na vidyate*

:79\2\32:yadṛcchayā copapannaṃ svargadvāramapāvṛtam,
sukhinaḥ kṣatriyāḥ pārtha labhante yuddhamīdṛśam*

:80\2\33:atha cettvamimaṃ dharmyaṃ saṅgrāmaṃ na kariṣyasi,
tataḥ svadharmaṃ kīrtiṃ ca hitvā pāpamavāpsyasi*

:81\2\34:akīrtiṃ cāpi bhūtāni kathayiṣyanti te'vyayām,
sambhāvitasya cākīrtirmaraṇādatiricyate*

:82\2\35:bhayādraṇāduparataṃ maṃsyante tvāṃ mahārathāḥ,
yeṣāṃ ca tvaṃ bahumato bhūtvā yāsyasi lāghavam*

:83\2\36:avācyavādāṃśca bahūnvadiṣyanti tavāhitāḥ,
nindantastava sāmarthyaṃ tato duḥkhataraṃ nu kim*

:84\2\37:hato vā prāpsyasi svargaṃ jitvā vā bhokṣyase mahīm,
tasmāduttiṣṭha kaunteya yuddhāya kṛtaniścayaḥ*

:85\2\38:sukhaduḥkhe same kṛtvā lābhālābhau jayājayau,
tato yuddhāya yujyasva naivaṃ pāpamavāpsyasi*

:86\2\39:eṣā te'bhihitā sāṅkhye buddhiryoge tvimāṃ śṛṇu,
buddhyā yukto yayā pārtha karmabandhaṃ prahāsyasi*

:87\2\40:nehābhikramanāśo'sti pratyavāyo na vidyate,
svalpamapyasya dharmasya trāyate mahato bhayāt*

:88\2\41:vyavasāyātmikā buddhirekeha kurunandana,
bahuśākhā hyanantāśca buddhayo'vyavasāyinām*

:89\2\42:yāmimāṃ puṣpitāṃ vācaṃ pravadantyavipaścitaḥ,
vedavādaratāḥ pārtha nānyadastīti vādinaḥ*

:90\2\43:kāmātmānaḥ svargaparā janmakarmaphalapradām,
kriyāviśeṣabahulāṃ bhogaiśvaryagatiṃ prati*

:91\2\44:bhogaiśvaryaprasaktānāṃ tayāpahṛtacetasām,
vyavasāyātmikā buddhiḥ samādhau na vidhīyate*

:92\2\45:traiguṇyaviṣayā vedā nistraiguṇyo bhavārjuna,
nirdvandvo nityasattvastho niryogakṣema ātmavān*

:93\2\46:yāvānartha udapāne sarvataḥ samplutodake,
tāvānsarveṣu vedeṣu brāhmaṇasya vijānataḥ*

:94\2\47:karmaṇyevādhikāraste mā phaleṣu kadācana,
mā karmaphalaheturbhūrmā te saṅgo'stvakarmaṇi*

:95\2\48:yogasthaḥ kuru karmāṇi saṅgaṃ tyaktvā dhanañjaya,
siddhyasiddhyoḥ samo bhūtvā samatvaṃ yoga ucyate*

:96\2\49:dūreṇa hyavaraṃ karma buddhiyogāddhanañjaya,
buddhau śaraṇamanviccha kṛpaṇāḥ phalahetavaḥ*

:97\2\50:buddhiyukto jahātīha ubhe sukṛtaduṣkṛte,
tasmādyogāya yujyasva yogaḥ karmasu kauśalam*

:98\2\51:karmajaṃ buddhiyuktā hi phalaṃ tyaktvā manīṣiṇaḥ,
janmabandhavinirmuktāḥ padaṃ gacchantyanāmayam*

:99\2\52:yadā te mohakalilaṃ buddhirvyatitariṣyati,
tadā gantāsi nirvedaṃ śrotavyasya śrutasya ca*

:100\2\53:śrutivipratipannā te yadā sthāsyati niścalā,
samādhāvacalā buddhistadā yogamavāpsyasi*

arjuna uvāca,

:101\2\54:sthitaprajñasya kā bhāṣā samādhisthasya keśava,
sthitadhīḥ kiṃ prabhāṣeta kimāsīta vrajeta kim*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:102\2\55:prajahāti yadā kāmānsarvānpārtha manogatān,
ātmanyevātmanā tuṣṭaḥ sthitaprajñastadocyate*

:103\2\56:duḥkheṣvanudvignamanāḥ sukheṣu vigataspṛhaḥ,
vītarāgabhayakrodhaḥ sthitadhīrmunirucyate*

:104\2\57:yaḥ sarvatrānabhisnehastattatprāpya śubhāśubham,
nābhinandati na dveṣṭi tasya prajñā pratiṣṭhitā*

:105\2\58:yadā saṃharate cāyaṃ kūrmo'ṅgānīva sarvaśaḥ,
indriyāṇīndriyārthebhyastasya prajñā pratiṣṭhitā*

:106\2\59:viṣayā vinivartante nirāhārasya dehinaḥ,
rasavarjaṃ raso'pyasya paraṃ dṛṣṭvā nivartate*

:107\2\60:yatato hyapi kaunteya puruṣasya vipaścitaḥ,
indriyāṇi pramāthīni haranti prasabhaṃ manaḥ*

:108\2\61:tāni sarvāṇi saṃyamya yukta āsīta matparaḥ,
vaśe hi yasyendriyāṇi tasya prajñā pratiṣṭhitā*

:109\2\62:dhyāyato viṣayānpuṃsaḥ saṅgasteṣūpajāyate,
saṅgātsañjāyate kāmaḥ kāmātkrodho'bhijāyate*

:110\2\63:krodhādbhavati sammohaḥ sammohātsmṛtivibhramaḥ,
smṛtibhraṃśād buddhināśo buddhināśātpraṇaśyati*

rāgadveṣavimuktaistu viṣayānindriyaiścaran, 
ātmavaśyairvidheyātmā prasādamadhigacchati :111\2\64:*

:112\2\65:prasāde sarvaduḥkhānāṃ hānirasyopajāyate,
prasannacetaso hyāśu buddhiḥ paryavatiṣṭhate*

:113\2\66:nāsti buddhirayuktasya na cāyuktasya bhāvanā,
na cābhāvayataḥ śāntiraśāntasya kutaḥ sukham*

:114\2\67:indriyāṇāṃ hi caratāṃ yanmano'nuvidhīyate,
tadasya harati prajñāṃ vāyurnāvamivāmbhasi*

:115\2\68:tasmādyasya mahābāho nigṛhītāni sarvaśaḥ,
indriyāṇīndriyārthebhyastasya prajñā pratiṣṭhitā*

:116\2\69:yā niśā sarvabhūtānāṃ tasyāṃ jāgarti saṃyamī,
yasyāṃ jāgrati bhūtāni sā niśā paśyato muneḥ*

:117\2\70:āpūryamāṇamacalapratiṣṭhaṃ samudramāpaḥ praviśanti yadvat,
tadvatkāmā yaṃ praviśanti sarve sa śāntimāpnoti na kāmakāmī*

:118\2\71:vihāya kāmānyaḥ sarvānpumāṃścarati niḥspṛhaḥ,
nirmamo nirahaṅkāraḥ sa śāntimadhigacchati*

:119\2\72:eṣā brāhmī sthitiḥ pārtha naināṃ prāpya vimuhyati,
sthitvāsyāmantakāle'pi brahmanirvāṇamṛcchati*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde, 
sāṅkhyayogo nāma dvitīyo'dhyāyaḥ*0*:2:*0*

:3:*Karmayogaḥ*

atha tṛtīyo'dhyāyaḥ, karmayogaḥ

arjuna uvāca,

:120\3\1:jyāyasī cetkarmaṇaste matā buddhirjanārdana,
tatkiṃ karmaṇi ghore māṃ niyojayasi keśava*

:121\3\2:vyāmiśreṇeva vākyena buddhiṃ mohayasīva me,
tadekaṃ vada niścitya yena śreyo'hamāpnuyām*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:122\3\3:loke'smin dvividhā niṣṭhā purā proktā mayānagha,
jñānayogena sāṅkhyānāṃ karmayogena yoginām*

:123\3\4:na karmaṇāmanārambhānnaiṣkarmyaṃ puruṣo'śnute,
na ca saṃnyasanādeva siddhiṃ samadhigacchati*

:124\3\5:na hi kaścitkṣaṇamapi jātu tiṣṭhatyakarmakṛt,
kāryate hyavaśaḥ karma sarvaḥ prakṛtijairguṇaiḥ*

:125\3\6:karmendriyāṇi saṃyamya ya āste manasā smaran,
indriyārthānvimūḍhātmā mithyācāraḥ sa ucyate*

:126\3\7:yastvindriyāṇi manasā niyamyārabhate'rjuna,
karmendriyaiḥ karmayogamasaktaḥ sa viśiṣyate*

:127\3\8:niyataṃ kuru karma tvaṃ karma jyāyo hyakarmaṇaḥ,
śarīrayātrāpi ca te na prasiddhyedakarmaṇaḥ*

:128\3\9:yajñārthātkarmaṇo'nyatra loko'yaṃ karmabandhanaḥ,
tadarthaṃ karma kaunteya muktasaṅgaḥ samācara*

:129\3\10:sahayajñāḥ prajāḥ sṛṣṭvā purovāca prajāpatiḥ,
anena prasaviṣyadhvameṣa vo'stviṣṭakāmadhuk*

:130\3\11:devānbhāvayatānena te devā bhāvayantu vaḥ,
parasparaṃ bhāvayantaḥ śreyaḥ paramavāpsyatha*

:131\3\12:iṣṭānbhogānhi vo devā dāsyante yajñabhāvitāḥ,
tairdattānapradāyaibhyo yo bhuṅkte stena eva saḥ*

:132\3\13:yajñaśiṣṭāśinaḥ santo mucyante sarvakilbiṣaiḥ,
bhuñjate te tvaghaṃ pāpā ye pacantyātmakāraṇāt*

:133\3\14:annādbhavanti bhūtāni parjanyādannasambhavaḥ,
yajñādbhavati parjanyo yajñaḥ karmasamudbhavaḥ*

:134\3\15:karma brahmodbhavaṃ viddhi brahmākṣarasamudbhavam,
tasmātsarvagataṃ brahma nityaṃ yajñe pratiṣṭhitam*

:135\3\16:evaṃ pravartitaṃ cakraṃ nānuvartayatīha yaḥ,
aghāyurindriyārāmo moghaṃ pārtha sa jīvati*

:136\3\17:yastvātmaratireva syādātmatṛptaśca mānavaḥ,
ātmanyeva ca santuṣṭastasya kāryaṃ na vidyate*

:137\3\18:naiva tasya kṛtenārtho nākṛteneha kaścana,
na cāsya sarvabhūteṣu kaścidarthavyapāśrayaḥ*

:138\3\19:tasmādasaktaḥ satataṃ kāryaṃ karma samācara,
asakto hyācarankarma paramāpnoti pūruṣaḥ*

:139\3\20:karmaṇaiva hi saṃsiddhimāsthitā janakādayaḥ,
lokasaṅgrahamevāpi sampaśyankartumarhasi*

:140\3\21:yadyadācarati śreṣṭhastattadevetaro janaḥ,
sa yatpramāṇaṃ kurute lokastadanuvartate*

:141\3\22:na me pārthāsti kartavyaṃ triṣu lokeṣu kiñcana,
nānavāptamavāptavyaṃ varta eva ca karmaṇi*

:142\3\23:yadi hyahaṃ na varteyaṃ jātu karmaṇyatandritaḥ,
mama vartmānuvartante manuṣyāḥ pārtha sarvaśaḥ*

:143\3\24:utsīdeyurime lokā na kuryāṃ karma cedaham,
saṅkarasya ca kartā syāmupahanyāmimāḥ prajāḥ*

:144\3\25:saktāḥ karmaṇyavidvāṃso yathā kurvanti bhārata,
kuryādvidvāṃstathāsaktaścikīrṣurlokasaṅgraham*

:145\3\26:na buddhibhedaṃ janayedajñānāṃ karmasaṅginām,
joṣayetsarvakarmāṇi vidvānyuktaḥ samācaran*

:146\3\27:prakṛteḥ kriyamāṇāni guṇaiḥ karmāṇi sarvaśaḥ,
ahaṅkāravimūḍhātmā kartāhamiti manyate*

:147\3\28:tattvavittu mahābāho guṇakarmavibhāgayoḥ,
guṇā guṇeṣu vartanta iti matvā na sajjate*

:148\3\29:prakṛterguṇasammūḍhāḥ sajjante guṇakarmasu,
tānakṛtsnavido mandānkṛtsnavinna vicālayet*

:149\3\30:mayi sarvāṇi karmāṇi saṃnyasyādhyātmacetasā,
nirāśīrnirmamo bhūtvā yudhyasva vigatajvaraḥ*

:150\3\31:ye me matamidaṃ nityamanutiṣṭhanti mānavāḥ,
śraddhāvanto'nasūyanto mucyante te'pi karmabhiḥ*

:151\3\32:ye tvetadabhyasūyanto nānutiṣṭhanti me matam,
sarvajñānavimūḍhāṃstānviddhi naṣṭānacetasaḥ*

:152\3\33:sadṛśaṃ ceṣṭate svasyāḥ prakṛterjñānavānapi,
prakṛtiṃ yānti bhūtāni nigrahaḥ kiṃ kariṣyati*

:153\3\34:indriyasyendriyasyārthe rāgadveṣau vyavasthitau,
tayorna vaśamāgacchettau hyasya paripanthinau*

:154\3\35:śreyānsvadharmo viguṇaḥ paradharmātsvanuṣṭhitāt,
svadharme nidhanaṃ śreyaḥ paradharmo bhayāvahaḥ*

arjuna uvāca,

:155\3\36:atha kena prayukto'yaṃ pāpaṃ carati pūruṣaḥ,
anicchannapi vārṣṇeya balādiva niyojitaḥ*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:156\3\37:kāma eṣa krodha eṣa rajoguṇasamudbhavaḥ,
mahāśano mahāpāpmā viddhyenamiha vairiṇam*

:157\3\38:dhūmenāvriyate vahniryathādarśo malena ca,
yatholbenāvṛto garbhastathā tenedamāvṛtam*

:158\3\39:āvṛtaṃ jñānametena jñānino nityavairiṇā,
kāmarūpeṇa kaunteya duṣpūreṇānalena ca*

:159\3\40:indriyāṇi mano buddhirasyādhiṣṭhānamucyate,
etairvimohayatyeṣa jñānamāvṛtya dehinam*

:160\3\41:tasmāttvamindriyāṇyādau niyamya bharatarṣabha,
pāpmānaṃ prajahi hyenaṃ jñānavijñānanāśanam*

:161\3\42:indriyāṇi parāṇyāhurindriyebhyaḥ paraṃ manaḥ,
manasastu parā buddhiryo buddheḥ paratastu saḥ*

:162\3\43:evaṃ buddheḥ paraṃ buddhvā saṃstabhyātmānamātmanā,
jahi śatruṃ mahābāho kāmarūpaṃ durāsadam*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde,
karmayogo nāma tṛtīyo'dhyāyaḥ*0*:3:*0*

:4:*Jñānakarmasaṃnyāsayogaḥ*

atha caturtho'dhyāyaḥ, jñānakarmasaṃnyāsayogaḥ

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:163\4\1:imaṃ vivasvate yogaṃ proktavānahamavyayam,
vivasvānmanave prāha manurikṣvākave'bravīt*

:164\4\2:evaṃ paramparāprāptamimaṃ rājarṣayo viduḥ,
sa kāleneha mahatā yogo naṣṭaḥ parantapa*

:165\4\3:sa evāyaṃ mayā te'dya yogaḥ proktaḥ purātanaḥ,
bhakto'si me sakhā ceti rahasyaṃ hyetaduttamam*

arjuna uvāca,

:166\4\4:aparaṃ bhavato janma paraṃ janma vivasvataḥ,
kathametadvijānīyāṃ tvamādau proktavāniti*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:167\4\5:bahūni me vyatītāni janmāni tava cārjuna,
tānyahaṃ veda sarvāṇi na tvaṃ vettha parantapa*

:168\4\6:ajo'pi sannavyayātmā bhūtānāmīśvaro'pi san,
prakṛtiṃ svāmadhiṣṭhāya sambhavāmyātmamāyayā*

:169\4\7:yadā yadā hi dharmasya glānirbhavati bhārata,
abhyutthānamadharmasya tadātmānaṃ sṛjāmyaham*

:170\4\8:paritrāṇāya sādhūnāṃ vināśāya ca duṣkṛtām,
dharmasaṃsthāpanārthāya sambhavāmi yuge yuge*

:171\4\9:janma karma ca me divyamevaṃ yo vetti tattvataḥ,
tyaktvā dehaṃ punarjanma naiti māmeti so'rjuna*

:172\4\10:vītarāgabhayakrodhā manmayā māmupāśritāḥ,
bahavo jñānatapasā pūtā madbhāvamāgatāḥ*

:173\4\11:ye yathā māṃ prapadyante tāṃstathaiva bhajāmyaham,
mama vartmānuvartante manuṣyāḥ pārtha sarvaśaḥ*

:174\4\12:kāṅkṣantaḥ karmaṇāṃ siddhiṃ yajanta iha devatāḥ,
kṣipraṃ hi mānuṣe loke siddhirbhavati karmajā*

:175\4\13:cāturvarṇyaṃ mayā sṛṣṭaṃ guṇakarmavibhāgaśaḥ,
tasya kartāramapi māṃ viddhyakartāramavyayam*

:176\4\14:na māṃ karmāṇi limpanti na me karmaphale spṛhā,
iti māṃ yo'bhijānāti karmabhirna sa badhyate*

:177\4\15:evaṃ jñātvā kṛtaṃ karma pūrvairapi mumukṣubhiḥ,
kuru karmaiva tasmāttvaṃ pūrvaiḥ pūrvataraṃ kṛtam*

:178\4\16:kiṃ karma kimakarmeti kavayo'pyatra mohitāḥ,
tatte karma pravakṣyāmi yajjñātvā mokṣyase'śubhāt*

:179\4\17:karmaṇo hyapi boddhavyaṃ boddhavyaṃ ca vikarmaṇaḥ,
akarmaṇaśca boddhavyaṃ gahanā karmaṇo gatiḥ*

:180\4\18:karmaṇyakarma yaḥ paśyedakarmaṇi ca karma yaḥ,
sa buddhimānmanuṣyeṣu sa yuktaḥ kṛtsnakarmakṛt*

:181\4\19:yasya sarve samārambhāḥ kāmasaṅkalpavarjitāḥ,
jñānāgnidagdhakarmāṇaṃ tamāhuḥ paṇḍitaṃ budhāḥ*

:182\4\20:tyaktvā karmaphalāsaṅgaṃ nityatṛpto nirāśrayaḥ,
karmaṇyabhipravṛtto'pi naiva kiñcitkaroti saḥ*

:183\4\21:nirāśīryatacittātmā tyaktasarvaparigrahaḥ,
śārīraṃ kevalaṃ karma kurvannāpnoti kilbiṣam*

:184\4\22:yadṛcchālābhasantuṣṭo dvandvātīto vimatsaraḥ,
samaḥ siddhāvasiddhau ca kṛtvāpi na nibadhyate*

:185\4\23:gatasaṅgasya muktasya jñānāvasthitacetasaḥ,
yajñāyācarataḥ karma samagraṃ pravilīyate*

:186\4\24:brahmārpaṇaṃ brahma havirbrahmāgnau brahmaṇā hutam,
brahmaiva tena gantavyaṃ brahmakarmasamādhinā*

:187\4\25:daivamevāpare yajñaṃ yoginaḥ paryupāsate,
brahmāgnāvapare yajñaṃ yajñenaivopajuhvati*

:188\4\26:śrotrādīnīndriyāṇyanye saṃyamāgniṣu juhvati,
śabdādīnviṣayānanya indriyāgniṣu juhvati*

:189\4\27:sarvāṇīndriyakarmāṇi prāṇakarmāṇi cāpare,
ātmasaṃyamayogāgnau juhvati jñānadīpite*

:190\4\28:dravyayajñāstapoyajñā yogayajñāstathāpare,
svādhyāyajñānayajñāśca yatayaḥ saṃśitavratāḥ*

:191\4\29:apāne juhvati prāṇaṃ prāṇe'pānaṃ tathāpare,
prāṇāpānagatī ruddhvā prāṇāyāmaparāyaṇāḥ*

:192\4\30:apare niyatāhārāḥ prāṇānprāṇeṣu juhvati,
sarve'pyete yajñavido yajñakṣapitakalmaṣāḥ*

:193\4\31:yajñaśiṣṭāmṛtabhujo yānti brahma sanātanam,
nāyaṃ loko'styayajñasya kuto'nyaḥ kurusattama*

:194\4\32:evaṃ bahuvidhā yajñā vitatā brahmaṇo mukhe,
karmajānviddhi tānsarvānevaṃ jñātvā vimokṣyase*

:195\4\33:śreyāndravyamayādyajñājjñānayajñaḥ parantapa,
sarvaṃ karmākhilaṃ pārtha jñāne parisamāpyate*

:196\4\34:tadviddhi praṇipātena paripraśnena sevayā,
upadekṣyanti te jñānaṃ jñāninastattvadarśinaḥ*

:197\4\35:yajjñātvā na punarmohamevaṃ yāsyasi pāṇḍava,
yena bhūtānyaśeṣeṇa drakṣyasyātmanyatho mayi* 

:198\4\36:api cedasi pāpebhyaḥ sarvebhyaḥ pāpakṛttamaḥ,
sarvaṃ jñānaplavenaiva vṛjinaṃ santariṣyasi*

:199\4\37:yathaidhāṃsi samiddho'gnirbhasmasātkurute'rjuna,
jñānāgniḥ sarvakarmāṇi bhasmasātkurute tathā*

:200\4\38:na hi jñānena sadṛśaṃ pavitramiha vidyate,
tatsvayaṃ yogasaṃsiddhaḥ kālenātmani vindati*

:201\4\39:śraddhāvā~llabhate jñānaṃ tatparaḥ saṃyatendriyaḥ,
jñānaṃ labdhvā parāṃ śāntimacireṇādhigacchati*

:202\4\40:ajñaścāśraddadhānaśca saṃśayātmā vinaśyati,
nāyaṃ loko'sti na paro na sukhaṃ saṃśayātmanaḥ*

:203\4\41:yogasaṃnyastakarmāṇaṃ jñānasañchinnasaṃśayam,
ātmavantaṃ na karmāṇi nibadhnanti dhanañjaya*

:204\4\42:tasmādajñānasambhūtaṃ hṛtsthaṃ jñānāsinātmanaḥ,
chittvainaṃ saṃśayaṃ yogamātiṣṭhottiṣṭha bhārata*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde,
jñānakarmasaṃnyāsayogo nāma caturtho'dhyāyaḥ*0*:4:*0*

:5:*Saṃnyāsayogaḥ*

atha pañcamo'dhyāyaḥ, saṃnyāsayogaḥ

arjuna uvāca,

:205\5\1:saṃnyāsaṃ karmaṇāṃ kṛṣṇa punaryogaṃ ca śaṃsasi,
yacchreya etayorekaṃ tanme brūhi suniścitam*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:206\5\2:saṃnyāsaḥ karmayogaśca niḥśreyasakarāvubhau,
tayostu karmasaṃnyāsātkarmayogo viśiṣyate*

:207\5\3:jñeyaḥ sa nityasaṃnyāsī yo na dveṣṭi na kāṅkṣati,
nirdvandvo hi mahābāho sukhaṃ bandhātpramucyate*

:208\5\4:sāṅkhyayogau pṛthagbālāḥ pravadanti na paṇḍitāḥ,
ekamapyāsthitaḥ samyagubhayorvindate phalam*

:209\5\5:yatsāṅkhyaiḥ prāpyate sthānaṃ tadyogairapi gamyate,
ekaṃ sāṅkhyaṃ ca yogaṃ ca yaḥ paśyati sa paśyati*

:210\5\6:saṃnyāsastu mahābāho duḥkhamāptumayogataḥ,
yogayukto munirbrahma nacireṇādhigacchati*

:211\5\7:yogayukto viśuddhātmā vijitātmā jitendriyaḥ,
sarvabhūtātmabhūtātmā kurvannapi na lipyate*

:212\5\8:naiva kiñcitkaromīti yukto manyeta tattvavit,
paśyañśṛṇvanspṛśañjighrannaśnangacchansvapañśvasan*

:213\5\9:pralapanvisṛjangṛhṇannunmiṣannimiṣannapi,
indriyāṇīndriyārtheṣu vartanta iti dhārayan*

:214\5\10:brahmaṇyādhāya karmāṇi saṅgaṃ tyaktvā karoti yaḥ,
lipyate na sa pāpena padmapatramivāmbhasā*

:215\5\11:kāyena manasā buddhyā kevalairindriyairapi,
yoginaḥ karma kurvanti saṅgaṃ tyaktvātmaśuddhaye*

:216\5\12:yuktaḥ karmaphalaṃ tyaktvā śāntimāpnoti naiṣṭhikīm,
ayuktaḥ kāmakāreṇa phale sakto nibadhyate*

:217\5\13:sarvakarmāṇi manasā saṃnyasyāste sukhaṃ vaśī,
navadvāre pure dehī naiva kurvanna kārayan*

:218\5\14:na kartṛtvaṃ na karmāṇi lokasya sṛjati prabhuḥ,
na karmaphalasaṃyogaṃ svabhāvastu pravartate*

:219\5\15:nādatte kasyacitpāpaṃ na caiva sukṛtaṃ vibhuḥ,
ajñānenāvṛtaṃ jñānaṃ tena muhyanti jantavaḥ*

:220\5\16:jñānena tu tadajñānaṃ yeṣāṃ nāśitamātmanaḥ,
teṣāmādityavajjñānaṃ prakāśayati tatparam*

:221\5\17:tadbuddhayastadātmānastanniṣṭhāstatparāyaṇāḥ,
gacchantyapunarāvṛttiṃ jñānanirdhūtakalmaṣāḥ*

:222\5\18:vidyāvinayasampanne brāhmaṇe gavi hastini,
śuni caiva śvapāke ca paṇḍitāḥ samadarśinaḥ*

:223\5\19:ihaiva tairjitaḥ sargo yeṣāṃ sāmye sthitaṃ manaḥ,
nirdoṣaṃ hi samaṃ brahma tasmād brahmaṇi te sthitāḥ*

:224\5\20:na prahṛṣyetpriyaṃ prāpya nodvijetprāpya cāpriyam,
sthirabuddhirasammūḍho brahmavid brahmaṇi sthitaḥ*

:225\5\21:bāhyasparśeṣvasaktātmā vindatyātmani yatsukham,
sa brahmayogayuktātmā sukhamakṣayamaśnute*

:226\5\22:ye hi saṃsparśajā bhogā duḥkhayonaya eva te,
ādyantavantaḥ kaunteya na teṣu ramate budhaḥ*

:227\5\23:śaknotīhaiva yaḥ soḍhuṃ prākśarīravimokṣaṇāt,
kāmakrodhodbhavaṃ vegaṃ sa yuktaḥ sa sukhī naraḥ*

:228\5\24:yo'ntaḥsukho'ntarārāmastathāntarjyotireva yaḥ,
sa yogī brahmanirvāṇaṃ brahmabhūto'dhigacchati*

:229\5\25:labhante brahmanirvāṇamṛṣayaḥ kṣīṇakalmaṣāḥ,
chinnadvaidhā yatātmānaḥ sarvabhūtahite ratāḥ*

:230\5\26:kāmakrodhaviyuktānāṃ yatīnāṃ yatacetasām,
abhito brahmanirvāṇaṃ vartate viditātmanām*

:231\5\27:sparśānkṛtvā bahirbāhyāṃścakṣuścaivāntare bhruvoḥ,
prāṇāpānau samau kṛtvā nāsābhyantaracāriṇau*

:232\5\28:yatendriyamanobuddhirmunirmokṣaparāyaṇaḥ,
vigatecchābhayakrodho yaḥ sadā mukta eva saḥ*

:233\5\29:bhoktāraṃ yajñatapasāṃ sarvalokamaheśvaram,
suhṛdaṃ sarvabhūtānāṃ jñātvā māṃ śāntimṛcchati*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde,
saṃnyāsayogo nāma pañcamo'dhyāyaḥ*0*:5:*0*

:6:*Ātmasaṃyamayogaḥ*

atha ṣaṣṭho'dhyāyaḥ, ātmasaṃyamayogaḥ

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:234\6\1:anāśritaḥ karmaphalaṃ kāryaṃ karma karoti yaḥ,
sa saṃnyāsī ca yogī ca na niragnirna cākriyaḥ*

:235\6\2:yaṃ saṃnyāsamiti prāhuryogaṃ taṃ viddhi pāṇḍava,
na hyasaṃnyastasaṅkalpo yogī bhavati kaścana*

:236\6\3:ārurukṣormuneryogaṃ karma kāraṇamucyate,
yogārūḍhasya tasyaiva śamaḥ kāraṇamucyate*

:237\6\4:yadā hi nendriyārtheṣu na karmasvanuṣajjate,
sarvasaṅkalpasaṃnyāsī yogārūḍhastadocyate*

:238\6\5:uddharedātmanātmānaṃ nātmānamavasādayet,
ātmaiva hyātmano bandhurātmaiva ripurātmanaḥ*

:239\6\6:bandhurātmātmanastasya yenātmaivātmanā jitaḥ,
anātmanastu śatrutve vartetātmaiva śatruvat*

:240\6\7:jitātmanaḥ praśāntasya paramātmā samāhitaḥ,
śītoṣṇasukhaduḥkheṣu tathā mānāpamānayoḥ*

:241\6\8:jñānavijñānatṛptātmā kūṭastho vijitendriyaḥ,
yukta ityucyate yogī samaloṣṭāśmakāñcanaḥ*

:242\6\9:suhṛnmitrāryudāsīnamadhyasthadveṣyabandhuṣu,
sādhuṣvapi ca pāpeṣu samabuddhirviśiṣyate*

:243\6\10:yogī yuñjīta satatamātmānaṃ rahasi sthitaḥ,
ekākī yatacittātmā nirāśīraparigrahaḥ*

:244\6\11:śucau deśe pratiṣṭhāpya sthiramāsanamātmanaḥ,
nātyucchritaṃ nātinīcaṃ cailājinakuśottaram*

:245\6\12:tatraikāgraṃ manaḥ kṛtvā yatacittendriyakriyaḥ,
upaviśyāsane yuñjyādyogamātmaviśuddhaye*

:246\6\13:samaṃ kāyaśirogrīvaṃ dhārayannacalaṃ sthiraḥ,
samprekṣya nāsikāgraṃ svaṃ diśaścānavalokayan*

:247\6\14:praśāntātmā vigatabhīrbrahmacārivrate sthitaḥ,
manaḥ saṃyamya maccitto yukta āsīta matparaḥ*

:248\6\15:yuñjannevaṃ sadātmānaṃ yogī niyatamānasaḥ,
śāntiṃ nirvāṇaparamāṃ matsaṃsthāmadhigacchati*

:249\6\16:nātyaśnatastu yogo'sti na caikāntamanaśnataḥ,
na cātisvapnaśīlasya jāgrato naiva cārjuna*

:250\6\17:yuktāhāravihārasya yuktaceṣṭasya karmasu,
yuktasvapnāvabodhasya yogo bhavati duḥkhahā*

:251\6\18:yadā viniyataṃ cittamātmanyevāvatiṣṭhate,
niḥspṛhaḥ sarvakāmebhyo yukta ityucyate tadā*

:252\6\19:yathā dīpo nivātastho neṅgate sopamā smṛtā,
yogino yatacittasya yuñjato yogamātmanaḥ*

:253\6\20:yatroparamate cittaṃ niruddhaṃ yogasevayā,
yatra caivātmanātmānaṃ paśyannātmani tuṣyati*

:254\6\21:sukhamātyantikaṃ yattad buddhigrāhyamatīndriyam,
vetti yatra na caivāyaṃ sthitaścalati tattvataḥ*

:255\6\22:yaṃ labdhvā cāparaṃ lābhaṃ manyate nādhikaṃ tataḥ,
yasminsthito na duḥkhena guruṇāpi vicālyate*

:256\6\23:taṃ vidyād duḥkhasaṃyogaviyogaṃ yogasaṃjñitam,
sa niścayena yoktavyo yogo'nirviṇṇacetasā*

:257\6\24:saṅkalpaprabhavānkāmāṃstyaktvā sarvānaśeṣataḥ,
manasaivendriyagrāmaṃ viniyamya samantataḥ*

:258\6\25:śanaiḥ śanairuparamed buddhyā dhṛtigṛhītayā,
ātmasaṃsthaṃ manaḥ kṛtvā na kiñcidapi cintayet*

:259\6\26:yato yato niścarati manaścañcalamasthiram,
tatastato niyamyaitadātmanyeva vaśaṃ nayet*

:260\6\27:praśāntamanasaṃ hyenaṃ yoginaṃ sukhamuttamam,
upaiti śāntarajasaṃ brahmabhūtamakalmaṣam*

:261\6\28:yuñjannevaṃ sadātmānaṃ yogī vigatakalmaṣaḥ,
sukhena brahmasaṃsparśamatyantaṃ sukhamaśnute*

:262\6\29:sarvabhūtasthamātmānaṃ sarvabhūtāni cātmani,
īkṣate yogayuktātmā sarvatra samadarśanaḥ*

:263\6\30:yo māṃ paśyati sarvatra sarvaṃ ca mayi paśyati,
tasyāhaṃ na praṇaśyāmi sa ca me na praṇaśyati*

:264\6\31:sarvabhūtasthitaṃ yo māṃ bhajatyekatvamāsthitaḥ,
sarvathā vartamāno'pi sa yogī mayi vartate*

:265\6\32:ātmaupamyena sarvatra samaṃ paśyati yo'rjuna,
sukhaṃ vā yadi vā duḥkhaṃ sa yogī paramo mataḥ*

arjuna uvāca,

:266\6\33:yo'yaṃ yogastvayā proktaḥ sāmyena madhusūdana,
etasyāhaṃ na paśyāmi cañcalatvātsthitiṃ sthirām*

:267\6\34:cañcalaṃ hi manaḥ kṛṣṇa pramāthi balavad dṛḍham,
tasyāhaṃ nigrahaṃ manye vāyoriva suduṣkaram*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:268\6\35:asaṃśayaṃ mahābāho mano durnigrahaṃ calam,
abhyāsena tu kaunteya vairāgyeṇa ca gṛhyate*

:269\6\36:asaṃyatātmanā yogo duṣprāpa iti me matiḥ,
vaśyātmanā tu yatatā śakyo'vāptumupāyataḥ*

arjuna uvāca,

:270\6\37:ayatiḥ śraddhayopeto yogāccalitamānasaḥ,
aprāpya yogasaṃsiddhiṃ kāṃ gatiṃ kṛṣṇa gacchati*

:271\6\38:kaccinnobhayavibhraṣṭaśchinnābhramiva naśyati,
apratiṣṭho mahābāho vimūḍho brahmaṇaḥ pathi*

:272\6\39:etanme saṃśayaṃ kṛṣṇa chettumarhasyaśeṣataḥ,
tvadanyaḥ saṃśayasyāsya chettā na hyupapadyate*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:273\6\40:pārtha naiveha nāmutra vināśastasya vidyate,
na hi kalyāṇakṛtkaścid durgatiṃ tāta gacchati*

:274\6\41:prāpya puṇyakṛtāṃ lokānuṣitvā śāśvatīḥ samāḥ,
śucīnāṃ śrīmatāṃ gehe yogabhraṣṭo'bhijāyate*

:275\6\42:athavā yogināmeva kule bhavati dhīmatām,
etaddhi durlabhataraṃ loke janma yadīdṛśam*

:276\6\43:tatra taṃ buddhisaṃyogaṃ labhate paurvadehikam,
yatate ca tato bhūyaḥ saṃsiddhau kurunandana*

:277\6\44:pūrvābhyāsena tenaiva hriyate hyavaśo'pi saḥ,
jijñāsurapi yogasya śabdabrahmātivartate*

:278\6\45:prayatnādyatamānastu yogī saṃśuddhakilbiṣaḥ,
anekajanmasaṃsiddhastato yāti parāṃ gatim*

:279\6\46:tapasvibhyo'dhiko yogī jñānibhyo'pi mato'dhikaḥ,
karmibhyaścādhiko yogī tasmādyogī bhavārjuna*

:280\6\47:yogināmapi sarveṣāṃ madgatenāntarātmanā,
śraddhāvānbhajate yo māṃ sa me yuktatamo mataḥ*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde,
ātmasaṃyamayogo nāma ṣaṣṭho'dhyāyaḥ*0*:6:*0*

:7:*Jñānavijñānayogaḥ*

atha saptamo'dhyāyaḥ, jñānavijñānayogaḥ

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:281\7\1:mayyāsaktamanāḥ pārtha yogaṃ yuñjanmadāśrayaḥ,
asaṃśayaṃ samagraṃ māṃ yathā jñāsyasi tacchṛṇu*

:282\7\2:jñānaṃ te'haṃ savijñānamidaṃ vakṣyāmyaśeṣataḥ,
yajjñātvā neha bhūyo'nyajjñātavyamavaśiṣyate*

:283\7\3:manuṣyāṇāṃ sahasreṣu kaścidyatati siddhaye,
yatatāmapi siddhānāṃ kaścinmāṃ vetti tattvataḥ*

:284\7\4:bhūmirāpo'nalo vāyuḥ khaṃ mano buddhireva ca,
ahaṅkāra itīyaṃ me bhinnā prakṛtiraṣṭadhā*

:285\7\5:apareyamitastvanyāṃ prakṛtiṃ viddhi me parām,
jīvabhūtāṃ mahābāho yayedaṃ dhāryate jagat*

:286\7\6:etadyonīni bhūtāni sarvāṇītyupadhāraya,
ahaṃ kṛtsnasya jagataḥ prabhavaḥ pralayastathā*

:287\7\7:mattaḥ parataraṃ nānyatkiñcidasti dhanañjaya,
mayi sarvamidaṃ protaṃ sūtre maṇigaṇā iva*

:288\7\8:raso'hamapsu kaunteya prabhāsmi śaśisūryayoḥ,
praṇavaḥ sarvavedeṣu śabdaḥ khe pauruṣaṃ nṛṣu*

:289\7\9:puṇyo gandhaḥ pṛthivyāṃ ca tejaścāsmi vibhāvasau,
jīvanaṃ sarvabhūteṣu tapaścāsmi tapasviṣu*

:290\7\10:bījaṃ māṃ sarvabhūtānāṃ viddhi pārtha sanātanam,
buddhirbuddhimatāmasmi tejastejasvināmaham*

:291\7\11:balaṃ balavatāṃ cāhaṃ kāmarāgavivarjitam,
dharmāviruddho bhūteṣu kāmo'smi bharatarṣabha*

:292\7\12:ye caiva sāttvikā bhāvā rājasāstāmasāśca ye,
matta eveti tānviddhi na tvahaṃ teṣu te mayi*

:293\7\13:tribhirguṇamayairbhāvairebhiḥ sarvamidaṃ jagat,
mohitaṃ nābhijānāti māmebhyaḥ paramavyayam*

:294\7\14:daivī hyeṣā guṇamayī mama māyā duratyayā,
māmeva ye prapadyante māyāmetāṃ taranti te*

:295\7\15:na māṃ duṣkṛtino mūḍhāḥ prapadyante narādhamāḥ,
māyayāpahṛtajñānā āsuraṃ bhāvamāśritāḥ*

:296\7\16:caturvidhā bhajante māṃ janāḥ sukṛtino'rjuna,
ārto jijñāsurarthārthī jñānī ca bharatarṣabha*

:297\7\17:teṣāṃ jñānī nityayukta ekabhaktirviśiṣyate,
priyo hi jñānino'tyarthamahaṃ sa ca mama priyaḥ*

:298\7\18:udārāḥ sarva evaite jñānī tvātmaiva me matam,
āsthitaḥ sa hi yuktātmā māmevānuttamāṃ gatim*

:299\7\19:bahūnāṃ janmanāmante jñānavānmāṃ prapadyate,
vāsudevaḥ sarvamiti sa mahātmā sudurlabhaḥ*

:300\7\20:kāmaistaistairhṛtajñānāḥ prapadyante'nyadevatāḥ,
taṃ taṃ niyamamāsthāya prakṛtyā niyatāḥ svayā*

:301\7\21:yo yo yāṃ yāṃ tanuṃ bhaktaḥ śraddhayārcitumicchati,
tasya tasyācalāṃ śraddhāṃ tāmeva vidadhāmyaham*

:302\7\22:sa tayā śraddhayā yuktastasyārādhanamīhate,
labhate ca tataḥ kāmānmayaiva vihitānhi tān*

:303\7\23:antavattu phalaṃ teṣāṃ tadbhavatyalpamedhasām,
devāndevayajo yānti madbhaktā yānti māmapi*

:304\7\24:avyaktaṃ vyaktimāpannaṃ manyante māmabuddhayaḥ,
paraṃ bhāvamajānanto mamāvyayamanuttamam*

:305\7\25:nāhaṃ prakāśaḥ sarvasya yogamāyāsamāvṛtaḥ,
mūḍho'yaṃ nābhijānāti loko māmajamavyayam*

:306\7\26:vedāhaṃ samatītāni vartamānāni cārjuna,
bhaviṣyāṇi ca bhūtāni māṃ tu veda na kaścana*

:307\7\27:icchādveṣasamutthena dvandvamohena bhārata,
sarvabhūtāni sammohaṃ sarge yānti parantapa*

:308\7\28:yeṣāṃ tvantagataṃ pāpaṃ janānāṃ puṇyakarmaṇām,
te dvandvamohanirmuktā bhajante māṃ dṛḍhavratāḥ*

:309\7\29:jarāmaraṇamokṣāya māmāśritya yatanti ye,
te brahma tadviduḥ kṛtsnamadhyātmaṃ karma cākhilam*

:310\7\30:sādhibhūtādhidaivaṃ māṃ sādhiyajñaṃ ca ye viduḥ,
prayāṇakāle'pi ca māṃ te viduryuktacetasaḥ*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde,
jñānavijñānayogo nāma saptamo'dhyāyaḥ*0*:7:*0*

:8:*Akṣarabrahmayogaḥ*

atha aṣṭamo'dhyāyaḥ, akṣarabrahmayogaḥ

arjuna uvāca,

:311\8\1:kiṃ tad brahma kimadhyātmaṃ kiṃ karma puruṣottama,
adhibhūtaṃ ca kiṃ proktamadhidaivaṃ kimucyate*

:312\8\2:adhiyajñaḥ kathaṃ ko'tra dehe'sminmadhusūdana,
prayāṇakāle ca kathaṃ jñeyo'si niyatātmabhiḥ*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:313\8\3:akṣaraṃ brahma paramaṃ svabhāvo'dhyātmamucyate,
bhūtabhāvodbhavakaro visargaḥ karmasaṃjñitaḥ*

:314\8\4:adhibhūtaṃ kṣaro bhāvaḥ puruṣaścādhidaivatam,
adhiyajño'hamevātra dehe dehabhṛtāṃ vara*

:315\8\5:antakāle ca māmeva smaranmuktvā kalevaram,
yaḥ prayāti sa madbhāvaṃ yāti nāstyatra saṃśayaḥ*

:316\8\6:yaṃ yaṃ vāpi smaranbhāvaṃ tyajatyante kalevaram,
taṃ tamevaiti kaunteya sadā tadbhāvabhāvitaḥ*

:317\8\7:tasmātsarveṣu kāleṣu māmanusmara yudhya ca,
mayyarpitamanobuddhirmāmevaiṣyasyasaṃśayaḥ* orsaṃśayam

:318\8\8:abhyāsayogayuktena cetasā nānyagāminā,
paramaṃ puruṣaṃ divyaṃ yāti pārthānucintayan*

:319\8\9:kaviṃ purāṇamanuśāsitāra maṇoraṇīyaṃsamanusmaredyaḥ,
sarvasya dhātāramacintyarūpa mādityavarṇaṃ tamasaḥ parastāt*

:320\8\10:prayāṇakāle manasā'calena bhaktyā yukto yogabalena caiva,
bhruvormadhye prāṇamāveśya samyak sa taṃ paraṃ puruṣamupaiti divyam*

:321\8\11:yadakṣaraṃ vedavido vadanti viśanti yadyatayo vītarāgāḥ,
yadicchanto brahmacaryaṃ caranti tatte padaṃ saṅgraheṇa pravakṣye*

:322\8\12:sarvadvārāṇi saṃyamya mano hṛdi nirudhya ca,
mūrdhnyādhāyātmanaḥ prāṇamāsthito yogadhāraṇām*

:323\8\13:omityekākṣaraṃ brahma vyāharanmāmanusmaran,
yaḥ prayāti tyajandehaṃ sa yāti paramāṃ gatim*

:324\8\14:ananyacetāḥ satataṃ yo māṃ smarati nityaśaḥ,
tasyāhaṃ sulabhaḥ pārtha nityayuktasya yoginaḥ*

:325\8\15:māmupetya punarjanma duḥkhālayamaśāśvatam,
nāpnuvanti mahātmānaḥ saṃsiddhiṃ paramāṃ gatāḥ*

:326\8\16:ābrahmabhuvanāllokāḥ punarāvartino'rjuna,
māmupetya tu kaunteya punarjanma na vidyate*

:327\8\17:sahasrayugaparyantamaharyad brahmaṇo viduḥ,
rātriṃ yugasahasrāntāṃ te'horātravido janāḥ*

:328\8\18:avyaktād vyaktayaḥ sarvāḥ prabhavantyaharāgame,
rātryāgame pralīyante tatraivāvyaktasaṃjñake*

:329\8\19:bhūtagrāmaḥ sa evāyaṃ bhūtvā bhūtvā pralīyate,
rātryāgame'vaśaḥ pārtha prabhavatyaharāgame*

:330\8\20:parastasmāttu bhāvo'nyo'vyakto'vyaktātsanātanaḥ,
yaḥ sa sarveṣu bhūteṣu naśyatsu na vinaśyati*

:331\8\21:avyakto'kṣara ityuktastamāhuḥ paramāṃ gatim,
yaṃ prāpya na nivartante taddhāma paramaṃ mama*

:332\8\22:puruṣaḥ sa paraḥ pārtha bhaktyā labhyastvananyayā,
yasyāntaḥsthāni bhūtāni yena sarvamidaṃ tatam*

:333\8\23:yatra kāle tvanāvṛttimāvṛttiṃ caiva yoginaḥ,
prayātā yānti taṃ kālaṃ vakṣyāmi bharatarṣabha*

:334\8\24:agnirjyotirahaḥ śuklaḥ ṣaṇmāsā uttarāyaṇam,
tatra prayātā gacchanti brahma brahmavido janāḥ*

:335\8\25:dhūmo rātristathā kṛṣṇaḥ ṣaṇmāsā dakṣiṇāyanam,
tatra cāndramasaṃ jyotiryogī prāpya nivartate*

:336\8\26:śuklakṛṣṇe gatī hyete jagataḥ śāśvate mate,
ekayā yātyanāvṛttimanyayāvartate punaḥ*

:337\8\27:naite sṛtī pārtha jānanyogī muhyati kaścana,
tasmātsarveṣu kāleṣu yogayukto bhavārjuna*

:338\8\28:vedeṣu yajñeṣu tapaḥsu caiva dāneṣu yatpuṇyaphalaṃ pradiṣṭam,
atyeti tatsarvamidaṃ viditvā yogī paraṃ sthānamupaiti cādyam*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde,
akṣarabrahmayogo nāmāṣṭamo'dhyāyaḥ*0*:8:*0*

:9:*Rājavidyārājaguhyayogaḥ*

atha navamo'dhyāyaḥ, rājavidyārājaguhyayogaḥ

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:339\9\1:idaṃ tu te guhyatamaṃ pravakṣyāmyanasūyave,
jñānaṃ vijñānasahitaṃ yajjñātvā mokṣyase'śubhāt*

:340\9\2:rājavidyā rājaguhyaṃ pavitramidamuttamam,
pratyakṣāvagamaṃ dharmyaṃ susukhaṃ kartumavyayam*

:341\9\3:aśraddadhānāḥ puruṣā dharmasyāsya parantapa,
aprāpya māṃ nivartante mṛtyusaṃsāravartmani*

:342\9\4:mayā tatamidaṃ sarvaṃ jagadavyaktamūrtinā,
matsthāni sarvabhūtāni na cāhaṃ teṣvavasthitaḥ*

:343\9\5:na ca matsthāni bhūtāni paśya me yogamaiśvaram,
bhūtabhṛnna ca bhūtastho mamātmā bhūtabhāvanaḥ*

:344\9\6:yathākāśasthito nityaṃ vāyuḥ sarvatrago mahān,
tathā sarvāṇi bhūtāni matsthānītyupadhāraya*

:345\9\7:sarvabhūtāni kaunteya prakṛtiṃ yānti māmikām,
kalpakṣaye punastāni kalpādau visṛjāmyaham*

:346\9\8:prakṛtiṃ svāmavaṣṭabhya visṛjāmi punaḥ punaḥ,
bhūtagrāmamimaṃ kṛtsnamavaśaṃ prakṛtervaśāt*

:347\9\9:na ca māṃ tāni karmāṇi nibadhnanti dhanañjaya,
udāsīnavadāsīnamasaktaṃ teṣu karmasu*

:348\9\10:mayādhyakṣeṇa prakṛtiḥ sūyate sacarācaram,
hetunānena kaunteya jagadviparivartate*

:349\9\11:avajānanti māṃ mūḍhā mānuṣīṃ tanumāśritam,
paraṃ bhāvamajānanto mama bhūtamaheśvaram*

:350\9\12:moghāśā moghakarmāṇo moghajñānā vicetasaḥ,
rākṣasīmāsurīṃ caiva prakṛtiṃ mohinīṃ śritāḥ*

:351\9\13:mahātmānastu māṃ pārtha daivīṃ prakṛtimāśritāḥ,
bhajantyananyamanaso jñātvā bhūtādimavyayam*

:352\9\14:satataṃ kīrtayanto māṃ yatantaśca dṛḍhavratāḥ,
namasyantaśca māṃ bhaktyā nityayuktā upāsate*

:353\9\15:jñānayajñena cāpyanye yajanto māmupāsate,
ekatvena pṛthaktvena bahudhā viśvatomukham*

:354\9\16:ahaṃ kraturahaṃ yajñaḥ svadhāhamahamauṣadham,
mantro'hamahamevājyamahamagnirahaṃ hutam*

:355\9\17:pitāhamasya jagato mātā dhātā pitāmahaḥ,
vedyaṃ pavitramoṅkāra ṛksāma yajureva ca*

:356\9\18:gatirbhartā prabhuḥ sākṣī nivāsaḥ śaraṇaṃ suhṛt,
prabhavaḥ pralayaḥ sthānaṃ nidhānaṃ bījamavyayam*

:357\9\19:tapāmyahamahaṃ varṣaṃ nigṛhṇāmyutsṛjāmi ca,
amṛtaṃ caiva mṛtyuśca sadasaccāhamarjuna*

:358\9\20:traividyā māṃ somapāḥ pūtapāpā yajñairiṣṭvā svargatiṃ prārthayante,
te puṇyamāsādya surendraloka maśnanti divyāndivi devabhogān*

:359\9\21:te taṃ bhuktvā svargalokaṃ viśālaṃ kṣīṇe puṇye martyalokaṃ viśanti,
evaṃ trayīdharmamanuprapannā gatāgataṃ kāmakāmā labhante*

:360\9\22:ananyāścintayanto māṃ ye janāḥ paryupāsate,
teṣāṃ nityābhiyuktānāṃ yogakṣemaṃ vahāmyaham*

:361\9\23:ye'pyanyadevatā bhaktā yajante śraddhayānvitāḥ,
te'pi māmeva kaunteya yajantyavidhipūrvakam*

:362\9\24:ahaṃ hi sarvayajñānāṃ bhoktā ca prabhureva ca,
na tu māmabhijānanti tattvenātaścyavanti te*

:363\9\25:yānti devavratā devānpitṝnyānti pitṛvratāḥ,
bhūtāni yānti bhūtejyā yānti madyājino'pi mām*

:364\9\26:patraṃ puṣpaṃ phalaṃ toyaṃ yo me bhaktyā prayacchati,
tadahaṃ bhaktyupahṛtamaśnāmi prayatātmanaḥ*

:365\9\27:yatkaroṣi yadaśnāsi yajjuhoṣi dadāsi yat,
yattapasyasi kaunteya tatkuruṣva madarpaṇam*

:366\9\28:śubhāśubhaphalairevaṃ mokṣyase karmabandhanaiḥ,
saṃnyāsayogayuktātmā vimukto māmupaiṣyasi*

:367\9\29:samo'haṃ sarvabhūteṣu na me dveṣyo'sti na priyaḥ,
ye bhajanti tu māṃ bhaktyā mayi te teṣu cāpyaham*

:368\9\30:api cetsudurācāro bhajate māmananyabhāk,
sādhureva sa mantavyaḥ samyagvyavasito hi saḥ*

:369\9\31:kṣipraṃ bhavati dharmātmā śaśvacchāntiṃ nigacchati,
kaunteya pratijānīhi na me bhaktaḥ praṇaśyati*

:370\9\32:māṃ hi pārtha vyapāśritya ye'pi syuḥ pāpayonayaḥ,
striyo vaiśyāstathā śūdrāste'pi yānti parāṃ gatim*

:371\9\33:kiṃ punarbrāhmaṇāḥ puṇyā bhaktā rājarṣayastathā,
anityamasukhaṃ lokamimaṃ prāpya bhajasva mām*

:372\9\34:manmanā bhava madbhakto madyājī māṃ namaskuru,
māmevaiṣyasi yuktvaivamātmānaṃ matparāyaṇaḥ*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde,
rājavidyārājaguhyayogo nāma navamo'dhyāyaḥ*0*:9:*0*

:10:*Vibhūtiyogaḥ*

atha daśamo'dhyāyaḥ, vibhūtiyogaḥ

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:373\10\1:bhūya eva mahābāho śṛṇu me paramaṃ vacaḥ,
yatte'haṃ prīyamāṇāya vakṣyāmi hitakāmyayā*

:374\10\2:na me viduḥ suragaṇāḥ prabhavaṃ na maharṣayaḥ,
ahamādirhi devānāṃ maharṣīṇāṃ ca sarvaśaḥ*

:375\10\3:yo māmajamanādiṃ ca vetti lokamaheśvaram,
asammūḍhaḥ sa martyeṣu sarvapāpaiḥ pramucyate*

:376\10\4:buddhirjñānamasammohaḥ kṣamā satyaṃ damaḥ śamaḥ,
sukhaṃ duḥkhaṃ bhavo'bhāvo bhayaṃ cābhayameva ca*

:377\10\5:ahiṃsā samatā tuṣṭistapo dānaṃ yaśo'yaśaḥ,
bhavanti bhāvā bhūtānāṃ matta eva pṛthagvidhāḥ*

:378\10\6:maharṣayaḥ sapta pūrve catvāro manavastathā,
madbhāvā mānasā jātā yeṣāṃ loka imāḥ prajāḥ*

:379\10\7:etāṃ vibhūtiṃ yogaṃ ca mama yo vetti tattvataḥ,
so'vikampena yogena yujyate nātra saṃśayaḥ*

:380\10\8:ahaṃ sarvasya prabhavo mattaḥ sarvaṃ pravartate,
iti matvā bhajante māṃ budhā bhāvasamanvitāḥ*

:381\10\9:maccittā madgataprāṇā bodhayantaḥ parasparam,
kathayantaśca māṃ nityaṃ tuṣyanti ca ramanti ca*

:382\10\10:teṣāṃ satatayuktānāṃ bhajatāṃ prītipūrvakam,
dadāmi buddhiyogaṃ taṃ yena māmupayānti te*

:383\10\11:teṣāmevānukampārthamahamajñānajaṃ tamaḥ,
nāśayāmyātmabhāvastho jñānadīpena bhāsvatā*

arjuna uvāca,

:384\10\12:paraṃ brahma paraṃ dhāma pavitraṃ paramaṃ bhavān,
puruṣaṃ śāśvataṃ divyamādidevamajaṃ vibhum*

:385\10\13:āhustvāmṛṣayaḥ sarve devarṣirnāradastathā,
asito devalo vyāsaḥ svayaṃ caiva bravīṣi me*

:386\10\14:sarvametadṛtaṃ manye yanmāṃ vadasi keśava,
na hi te bhagavanvyaktiṃ vidurdevā na dānavāḥ*

:387\10\15:svayamevātmanātmānaṃ vettha tvaṃ puruṣottama,
bhūtabhāvana bhūteśa devadeva jagatpate*

:388\10\16:vaktumarhasyaśeṣeṇa divyā hyātmavibhūtayaḥ,
yābhirvibhūtibhirlokānimāṃstvaṃ vyāpya tiṣṭhasi*

:389\10\17:kathaṃ vidyāmahaṃ yogiṃstvāṃ sadā paricintayan,
keṣu keṣu ca bhāveṣu cintyo'si bhagavanmayā*

:390\10\18:vistareṇātmano yogaṃ vibhūtiṃ ca janārdana,
bhūyaḥ kathaya tṛptirhi śṛṇvato nāsti me'mṛtam*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:391\10\19:hanta te kathayiṣyāmi divyā hyātmavibhūtayaḥ,
prādhānyataḥ kuruśreṣṭha nāstyanto vistarasya me*

:392\10\20:ahamātmā guḍākeśa sarvabhūtāśayasthitaḥ,
ahamādiśca madhyaṃ ca bhūtānāmanta eva ca*

:393\10\21:ādityānāmahaṃ viṣṇurjyotiṣāṃ raviraṃśumān,
marīcirmarutāmasmi nakṣatrāṇāmahaṃ śaśī*

:394\10\22:vedānāṃ sāmavedo'smi devānāmasmi vāsavaḥ,
indriyāṇāṃ manaścāsmi bhūtānāmasmi cetanā*

:395\10\23:rudrāṇāṃ śaṅkaraścāsmi vitteśo yakṣarakṣasām,
vasūnāṃ pāvakaścāsmi meruḥ śikhariṇāmaham*

:396\10\24:purodhasāṃ ca mukhyaṃ māṃ viddhi pārtha bṛhaspatim,
senānīnāmahaṃ skandaḥ sarasāmasmi sāgaraḥ*

:397\10\25:maharṣīṇāṃ bhṛgurahaṃ girāmasmyekamakṣaram,
yajñānāṃ japayajño'smi sthāvarāṇāṃ himālayaḥ*

:398\10\26:aśvatthaḥ sarvavṛkṣāṇāṃ devarṣīṇāṃ ca nāradaḥ,
gandharvāṇāṃ citrarathaḥ siddhānāṃ kapilo muniḥ*

:399\10\27:uccaiḥśravasamaśvānāṃ viddhi māmamṛtodbhavam,
airāvataṃ gajendrāṇāṃ narāṇāṃ ca narādhipam*

:400\10\28:āyudhānāmahaṃ vajraṃ dhenūnāmasmi kāmadhuk,
prajanaścāsmi kandarpaḥ sarpāṇāmasmi vāsukiḥ*

:401\10\29:anantaścāsmi nāgānāṃ varuṇo yādasāmaham,
pitṝṇāmaryamā cāsmi yamaḥ saṃyamatāmaham*

:402\10\30:prahlādaścāsmi daityānāṃ kālaḥ kalayatāmaham,
mṛgāṇāṃ ca mṛgendro'haṃ vainateyaśca pakṣiṇām*

:403\10\31:pavanaḥ pavatāmasmi rāmaḥ śastrabhṛtāmaham,
jhaṣāṇāṃ makaraścāsmi srotasāmasmi jāhnavī*

:404\10\32:sargāṇāmādirantaśca madhyaṃ caivāhamarjuna,
adhyātmavidyā vidyānāṃ vādaḥ pravadatāmaham*

:405\10\33:akṣarāṇāmakāro'smi dvandvaḥ sāmāsikasya ca,
ahamevākṣayaḥ kālo dhātāhaṃ viśvatomukhaḥ*

:406\10\34:mṛtyuḥ sarvaharaścāhamudbhavaśca bhaviṣyatām,
kīrtiḥ śrīrvākca nārīṇāṃ smṛtirmedhā dhṛtiḥ kṣamā*

:407\10\35:bṛhatsāma tathā sāmnāṃ gāyatrī chandasāmaham,
māsānāṃ mārgaśīrṣo'hamṛtūnāṃ kusumākaraḥ*

:408\10\36:dyūtaṃ chalayatāmasmi tejastejasvināmaham,
jayo'smi vyavasāyo'smi sattvaṃ sattvavatāmaham*

:409\10\37:vṛṣṇīnāṃ vāsudevo'smi pāṇḍavānāṃ dhanañjayaḥ,
munīnāmapyahaṃ vyāsaḥ kavīnāmuśanā kaviḥ*

:410\10\38:daṇḍo damayatāmasmi nītirasmi jigīṣatām,
maunaṃ caivāsmi guhyānāṃ jñānaṃ jñānavatāmaham*

:411\10\39:yaccāpi sarvabhūtānāṃ bījaṃ tadahamarjuna,
na tadasti vinā yatsyānmayā bhūtaṃ carācaram*

:412\10\40:nānto'sti mama divyānāṃ vibhūtīnāṃ parantapa,
eṣa tūddeśataḥ prokto vibhūtervistaro mayā*

:413\10\41:yadyadvibhūtimatsattvaṃ śrīmadūrjitameva vā,
tattadevāvagaccha tvaṃ mama tejoṃ'śasambhavam*

:414\10\42:athavā bahunaitena kiṃ jñātena tavārjuna,
viṣṭabhyāhamidaṃ kṛtsnamekāṃśena sthito jagat*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde,
vibhūtiyogo nāma daśamo'dhyāyaḥ*0*:10:*0*

:11:*Viśvarūpadarśanayogaḥ*

athaikādaśo'dhyāyaḥ, viśvarūpadarśanayogaḥ

arjuna uvāca,

:415\11\1:madanugrahāya paramaṃ guhyamadhyātmasaṃjñitam,
yattvayoktaṃ vacastena moho'yaṃ vigato mama*

:416\11\2:bhavāpyayau hi bhūtānāṃ śrutau vistaraśo mayā,
tvattaḥ kamalapatrākṣa māhātmyamapi cāvyayam*

:417\11\3:evametadyathāttha tvamātmānaṃ parameśvara,
draṣṭumicchāmi te rūpamaiśvaraṃ puruṣottama*

:418\11\4:manyase yadi tacchakyaṃ mayā draṣṭumiti prabho,
yogeśvara tato me tvaṃ darśayātmānamavyayam*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:419\11\5:paśya me pārtha rūpāṇi śataśo'tha sahasraśaḥ,
nānāvidhāni divyāni nānāvarṇākṛtīni ca*

:420\11\6:paśyādityānvasūnrudrānaśvinau marutastathā,
bahūnyadṛṣṭapūrvāṇi paśyāścaryāṇi bhārata*

:421\11\7:ihaikasthaṃ jagatkṛtsnaṃ paśyādya sacarācaram,
mama dehe guḍākeśa yaccānyad draṣṭumicchasi*

:422\11\8:na tu māṃ śakyase draṣṭumanenaiva svacakṣuṣā,
divyaṃ dadāmi te cakṣuḥ paśya me yogamaiśvaram*

sañjaya uvāca,

:423\11\9:evamuktvā tato rājanmahāyogeśvaro hariḥ,
darśayāmāsa pārthāya paramaṃ rūpamaiśvaram*

:424\11\10:anekavaktranayanamanekādbhutadarśanam,
anekadivyābharaṇaṃ divyānekodyatāyudham*

:425\11\11:divyamālyāmbaradharaṃ divyagandhānulepanam,
sarvāścaryamayaṃ devamanantaṃ viśvatomukham*

:426\11\12:divi sūryasahasrasya bhavedyugapadutthitā,
yadi bhāḥ sadṛśī sā syādbhāsastasya mahātmanaḥ*

:427\11\13:tatraikasthaṃ jagatkṛtsnaṃ pravibhaktamanekadhā,
apaśyaddevadevasya śarīre pāṇḍavastadā*

:428\11\14:tataḥ sa vismayāviṣṭo hṛṣṭaromā dhanañjayaḥ,
praṇamya śirasā devaṃ kṛtāñjalirabhāṣata*

arjuna uvāca,

:429\11\15:paśyāmi devāṃstava deva dehe sarvāṃstathā bhūtaviśeṣasaṅghān,
brahmāṇamīśaṃ kamalāsanasthamṛṣīṃśca sarvānuragāṃśca divyān*

:430\11\16:anekabāhūdaravaktranetraṃ paśyāmi tvāṃ sarvato'nantarūpam,
nāntaṃ na madhyaṃ na punastavādiṃ paśyāmi viśveśvara viśvarūpa*

:431\11\17:kirīṭinaṃ gadinaṃ cakriṇaṃ ca tejorāśiṃ sarvato dīptimantam,
paśyāmi tvāṃ durnirīkṣyaṃ samantād dīptānalārkadyutimaprameyam*

:432\11\18:tvamakṣaraṃ paramaṃ veditavyaṃ tvamasya viśvasya paraṃ nidhānam,
tvamavyayaḥ śāśvatadharmagoptā sanātanastvaṃ puruṣo mato me*

:433\11\19:anādimadhyāntamanantavīryamanantabāhuṃ śaśisūryanetram,
paśyāmi tvāṃ dīptahutāśavaktraṃ svatejasā viśvamidaṃ tapantam*

:434\11\20:dyāvāpṛthivyoridamantaraṃ hi vyāptaṃ tvayaikena diśaśca sarvāḥ,
dṛṣṭvādbhutaṃ rūpamugraṃ tavedaṃ lokatrayaṃ pravyathitaṃ mahātman*

:435\11\21:amī hi tvāṃ surasaṅghā viśanti kecidbhītāḥ prāñjalayo gṛṇanti,
svastītyuktvā maharṣisiddhasaṅghāḥ stuvanti tvāṃ stutibhiḥ puṣkalābhiḥ*

:436\11\22:rudrādityā vasavo ye ca sādhyā viśve'śvinau marutaścoṣmapāśca,
gandharvayakṣāsurasiddhasaṅghā vīkṣante tvāṃ vismitāścaiva sarve*

:437\11\23:rūpaṃ mahatte bahuvaktranetraṃ mahābāho bahubāhūrupādam,
bahūdaraṃ bahudaṃṣṭrākarālaṃ dṛṣṭvā lokāḥ pravyathitāstathāham*

:438\11\24:nabhaḥspṛśaṃ dīptamanekavarṇaṃ vyāttānanaṃ dīptaviśālanetram,
dṛṣṭvā hi tvāṃ pravyathitāntarātmā dhṛtiṃ na vindāmi śamaṃ ca viṣṇo*

:439\11\25:daṃṣṭrākarālāni ca te mukhāni dṛṣṭvaiva kālānalasannibhāni,
diśo na jāne na labhe ca śarma prasīda deveśa jagannivāsa*

:440\11\26:amī ca tvāṃ dhṛtarāṣṭrasya putrāḥ sarve sahaivāvanipālasaṅghaiḥ,
bhīṣmo droṇaḥ sūtaputrastathāsau sahāsmadīyairapi yodhamukhyaiḥ*

:441\11\27:vaktrāṇi te tvaramāṇā viśanti daṃṣṭrākarālāni bhayānakāni,
kecidvilagnā daśanāntareṣu sandṛśyante cūrṇitairuttamāṅgaiḥ*

:442\11\28:yathā nadīnāṃ bahavo'mbuvegāḥ samudramevābhimukhā dravanti,
tathā tavāmī naralokavīrā viśanti vaktrāṇyabhivijvalanti*

:443\11\29:yathā pradīptaṃ jvalanaṃ pataṅgā viśanti nāśāya samṛddhavegāḥ,
tathaiva nāśāya viśanti lokāstavāpi vaktrāṇi samṛddhavegāḥ*

:444\11\30:lelihyase grasamānaḥ samantāllokānsamagrānvadanairjvaladbhiḥ,
tejobhirāpūrya jagatsamagraṃ bhāsastavogrāḥ pratapanti viṣṇo*

:445\11\31:ākhyāhi me ko bhavānugrarūpo namo'stu te devavara prasīda,
vijñātumicchāmi bhavantamādyaṃ na hi prajānāmi tava pravṛttim*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:446\11\32:kālo'smi lokakṣayakṛtpravṛddho lokānsamāhartumiha pravṛttaḥ,
ṛte'pi tvāṃ na bhaviṣyanti sarve ye'vasthitāḥ pratyanīkeṣu yodhāḥ*

:447\11\33:tasmāttvamuttiṣṭha yaśo labhasva jitvā śatrūn bhuṅkṣva rājyaṃ samṛddham,
mayaivaite nihatāḥ pūrvameva nimittamātraṃ bhava savyasācin*

:448\11\34:droṇaṃ ca bhīṣmaṃ ca jayadrathaṃ ca karṇaṃ tathānyānapi yodhavīrān,
mayā hatāṃstvaṃ jahi mā vyathiṣṭhā yudhyasva jetāsi raṇe sapatnān*

sañjaya uvāca,

:449\11\35:etacchrutvā vacanaṃ keśavasya kṛtāñjalirvepamānaḥ kirīṭī,
namaskṛtvā bhūya evāha kṛṣṇaṃ sagadgadaṃ bhītabhītaḥ praṇamya*

arjuna uvāca,

:450\11\36:sthāne hṛṣīkeśa tava prakīrtyā jagatprahṛṣyatyanurajyate ca,
rakṣāṃsi bhītāni diśo dravanti sarve namasyanti ca siddhasaṅghāḥ*

:451\11\37:kasmācca te na nameranmahātman garīyase brahmaṇo'pyādikartre,
ananta deveśa jagannivāsa tvamakṣaraṃ sadasattatparaṃ yat*

:452\11\38:tvamādidevaḥ puruṣaḥ purāṇastvamasya viśvasya paraṃ nidhānam,
vettāsi vedyaṃ ca paraṃ ca dhāma tvayā tataṃ viśvamanantarūpa*

:453\11\39:vāyuryamo'gnirvaruṇaḥ śaśāṅkaḥ prajāpatistvaṃ prapitāmahaśca,
namo namaste'stu sahasrakṛtvaḥ punaśca bhūyo'pi namo namaste*

:454\11\40:namaḥ purastādatha pṛṣṭhataste namo'stu te sarvata eva sarva,
anantavīryāmitavikramastvaṃ sarvaṃ samāpnoṣi tato'si sarvaḥ*

:455\11\41:sakheti matvā prasabhaṃ yaduktaṃ he kṛṣṇa he yādava he sakheti,
ajānatā mahimānaṃ tavedaṃ mayā pramādātpraṇayena vāpi*

:456\11\42:yaccāvahāsārthamasatkṛto'si vihāraśayyāsanabhojaneṣu,
eko'thavāpyacyuta tatsamakṣaṃ tatkṣāmaye tvāmahamaprameyam*

:457\11\43:pitāsi lokasya carācarasya tvamasya pūjyaśca gururgarīyān,
na tvatsamo'styabhyadhikaḥ kuto'nyo lokatraye'pyapratimaprabhāva*

:458\11\44:tasmātpraṇamya praṇidhāya kāyaṃ prasādaye tvāmahamīśamīḍyam,
piteva putrasya sakheva sakhyuḥ priyaḥ priyāyārhasi deva soḍhum*

:459\11\45:adṛṣṭapūrvaṃ hṛṣito'smi dṛṣṭvā bhayena ca pravyathitaṃ mano me,
tadeva me darśaya deva rūpaṃ prasīda deveśa jagannivāsa*

:460\11\46:kirīṭinaṃ gadinaṃ cakrahastaṃ icchāmi tvāṃ draṣṭumahaṃ tathaiva,
tenaiva rūpeṇa caturbhujena sahasrabāho bhava viśvamūrte*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:461\11\47:mayā prasannena tavārjunedaṃ rūpaṃ paraṃ darśitamātmayogāt,
tejomayaṃ viśvamanantamādyaṃ yanme tvadanyena na dṛṣṭapūrvam*

:462\11\48:na vedayajñādhyayanairna dānair na ca kriyābhirna tapobhirugraiḥ,
evaṃrūpaḥ śakya ahaṃ nṛloke draṣṭuṃ tvadanyena kurupravīra*

:463\11\49:mā te vyathā mā ca vimūḍhabhāvo dṛṣṭvā rūpaṃ ghoramīdṛṅmamedam,
vyapetabhīḥ prītamanāḥ punastvaṃ tadeva me rūpamidaṃ prapaśya*

sañjaya uvāca,

:464\11\50:ityarjunaṃ vāsudevastathoktvā svakaṃ rūpaṃ darśayāmāsa bhūyaḥ,
āśvāsayāmāsa ca bhītamenaṃ bhūtvā punaḥ saumyavapurmahātmā*

arjuna uvāca,

:465\11\51:dṛṣṭvedaṃ mānuṣaṃ rūpaṃ tava saumyaṃ janārdana,
idānīmasmi saṃvṛttaḥ sacetāḥ prakṛtiṃ gataḥ*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:466\11\52:sudurdarśamidaṃ rūpaṃ dṛṣṭavānasi yanmama,
devā apyasya rūpasya nityaṃ darśanakāṅkṣiṇaḥ*

:467\11\53:nāhaṃ vedairna tapasā na dānena na cejyayā,
śakya evaṃvidho draṣṭuṃ dṛṣṭavānasi māṃ yathā*

:468\11\54:bhaktyā tvananyayā śakya ahamevaṃvidho'rjuna,
jñātuṃ draṣṭuṃ ca tattvena praveṣṭuṃ ca parantapa*

:469\11\55:matkarmakṛnmatparamo madbhaktaḥ saṅgavarjitaḥ,
nirvairaḥ sarvabhūteṣu yaḥ sa māmeti pāṇḍava*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde,
viśvarūpadarśanayogo nāmaikādaśo'dhyāyaḥ*0*:11:*0*

:12:*Bhaktiyogaḥ*

atha dvādaśo'dhyāyaḥ, bhaktiyogaḥ

arjuna uvāca,

:470\12\1:evaṃ satatayuktā ye bhaktāstvāṃ paryupāsate,
ye cāpyakṣaramavyaktaṃ teṣāṃ ke yogavittamāḥ*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:471\12\2:mayyāveśya mano ye māṃ nityayuktā upāsate,
śraddhayā parayopetāḥ te me yuktatamā matāḥ*

:472\12\3:ye tvakṣaramanirdeśyamavyaktaṃ paryupāsate,
sarvatragamacintyañca kūṭasthamacalandhruvam*

:473\12\4:sanniyamyendriyagrāmaṃ sarvatra samabuddhayaḥ,
te prāpnuvanti māmeva sarvabhūtahite ratāḥ*

:474\12\5:kleśo'dhikatarasteṣāmavyaktāsaktacetasām,
avyaktā hi gatirduḥkhaṃ dehavadbhiravāpyate*

:475\12\6:ye tu sarvāṇi karmāṇi mayi saṃnyasya matparāḥ,
ananyenaiva yogena māṃ dhyāyanta upāsate*

:476\12\7:teṣāmahaṃ samuddhartā mṛtyusaṃsārasāgarāt,
bhavāmi nacirātpārtha mayyāveśitacetasām*

:477\12\8:mayyeva mana ādhatsva mayi buddhiṃ niveśaya,
nivasiṣyasi mayyeva ata ūrdhvaṃ na saṃśayaḥ*

:478\12\9:atha cittaṃ samādhātuṃ na śaknoṣi mayi sthiram,
abhyāsayogena tato māmicchāptuṃ dhanañjaya*

:479\12\10:abhyāse'pyasamartho'si matkarmaparamo bhava,
madarthamapi karmāṇi kurvansiddhimavāpsyasi*

:480\12\11:athaitadapyaśakto'si kartuṃ madyogamāśritaḥ,
sarvakarmaphalatyāgaṃ tataḥ kuru yatātmavān*

:481\12\12:śreyo hi jñānamabhyāsājjñānāddhyānaṃ viśiṣyate,
dhyānātkarmaphalatyāgastyāgācchāntiranantaram*

:482\12\13:adveṣṭā sarvabhūtānāṃ maitraḥ karuṇa eva ca,
nirmamo nirahaṅkāraḥ samaduḥkhasukhaḥ kṣamī*

:483\12\14:santuṣṭaḥ satataṃ yogī yatātmā dṛḍhaniścayaḥ,
mayyarpitamanobuddhiryo madbhaktaḥ sa me priyaḥ*

:484\12\15:yasmānnodvijate loko lokānnodvijate ca yaḥ,
harṣāmarṣabhayodvegairmukto yaḥ sa ca me priyaḥ*

:485\12\16:anapekṣaḥ śucirdakṣa udāsīno gatavyathaḥ,
sarvārambhaparityāgī yo madbhaktaḥ sa me priyaḥ*

:486\12\17:yo na hṛṣyati na dveṣṭi na śocati na kāṅkṣati,
śubhāśubhaparityāgī bhaktimānyaḥ sa me priyaḥ*

:487\12\18:samaḥ śatrau ca mitre ca tathā mānāpamānayoḥ,
śītoṣṇasukhaduḥkheṣu samaḥ saṅgavivarjitaḥ*

:488\12\19:tulyanindāstutirmaunī santuṣṭo yena kenacit,
aniketaḥ sthiramatirbhaktimānme priyo naraḥ*

:489\12\20:ye tu dharmyāmṛtamidaṃ yathoktaṃ paryupāsate,
śraddadhānā matparamā bhaktāste'tīva me priyāḥ*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde,
bhaktiyogo nāma dvādaśo'dhyāyaḥ*0*:12:*0*

:13:*Kṣetrakṣetrajñavibhāgayogaḥ*

atha trayodaśo'dhyāyaḥ, kṣetrakṣetrajñavibhāgayogaḥ

arjuna uvāca,

:490\13\1:prakṛtiṃ puruṣaṃ caiva kṣetraṃ kṣetrajñameva ca,
etadveditumicchāmi jñānaṃ jñeyaṃ ca keśava*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:491\13\2:idaṃ śarīraṃ kaunteya kṣetramityabhidhīyate,
etadyo vetti taṃ prāhuḥ kṣetrajña iti tadvidaḥ*

:492\13\3:kṣetrajñaṃ cāpi māṃ viddhi sarvakṣetreṣu bhārata,
kṣetrakṣetrajñayorjñānaṃ yattajjñānaṃ mataṃ mama*

:493\13\4:tatkṣetraṃ yacca yādṛkca yadvikāri yataśca yat,
sa ca yo yatprabhāvaśca tatsamāsena me śṛṇu*

:494\13\5:ṛṣibhirbahudhā gītaṃ chandobhirvividhaiḥ pṛthak,
brahmasūtrapadaiścaiva hetumadbhirviniścitaiḥ*

:495\13\6:mahābhūtānyahaṅkāro buddhiravyaktameva ca,
indriyāṇi daśaikaṃ ca pañca cendriyagocarāḥ*

:496\13\7:icchā dveṣaḥ sukhaṃ duḥkhaṃ saṅghātaścetanā dhṛtiḥ,
etatkṣetraṃ samāsena savikāramudāhṛtam*

:497\13\8:amānitvamadambhitvamahiṃsā kṣāntirārjavam,
ācāryopāsanaṃ śaucaṃ sthairyamātmavinigrahaḥ*

:498\13\9:indriyārtheṣu vairāgyamanahaṅkāra eva ca,
janmamṛtyujarāvyādhiduḥkhadoṣānudarśanam*

:499\13\10:asaktiranabhiṣvaṅgaḥ putradāragṛhādiṣu,
nityaṃ ca samacittatvamiṣṭāniṣṭopapattiṣu*

:500\13\11:mayi cānanyayogena bhaktiravyabhicāriṇī,
viviktadeśasevitvamaratirjanasaṃsadi*

:501\13\12:adhyātmajñānanityatvaṃ tattvajñānārthadarśanam,
etajjñānamiti proktamajñānaṃ yadato'nyathā*

:502\13\13:jñeyaṃ yattatpravakṣyāmi yajjñātvāmṛtamaśnute,
anādimatparaṃ brahma na sattannāsaducyate*

:503\13\14:sarvataḥ pāṇipādaṃ tatsarvato'kṣiśiromukham,
sarvataḥ śrutimalloke sarvamāvṛtya tiṣṭhati*

:504\13\15:sarvendriyaguṇābhāsaṃ sarvendriyavivarjitam,
asaktaṃ sarvabhṛccaiva nirguṇaṃ guṇabhoktṛ ca*

:505\13\16:bahirantaśca bhūtānāmacaraṃ carameva ca,
sūkṣmatvāttadavijñeyaṃ dūrasthaṃ cāntike ca tat*

:506\13\17:avibhaktaṃ ca bhūteṣu vibhaktamiva ca sthitam,
bhūtabhartṛ ca tajjñeyaṃ grasiṣṇu prabhaviṣṇu ca*

:507\13\18:jyotiṣāmapi tajjyotistamasaḥ paramucyate,
jñānaṃ jñeyaṃ jñānagamyaṃ hṛdi sarvasya viṣṭhitam*

:508\13\19:iti kṣetraṃ tathā jñānaṃ jñeyaṃ coktaṃ samāsataḥ,
madbhakta etadvijñāya madbhāvāyopapadyate*

:509\13\20:prakṛtiṃ puruṣaṃ caiva viddhyanādī ubhāvapi,
vikārāṃśca guṇāṃścaiva viddhi prakṛtisambhavān*

:510\13\21:kāryakāraṇakartṛtve hetuḥ prakṛtirucyate,
puruṣaḥ sukhaduḥkhānāṃ bhoktṛtve heturucyate*

:511\13\22:puruṣaḥ prakṛtistho hi bhuṅkte prakṛtijānguṇān,
kāraṇaṃ guṇasaṅgo'sya sadasadyonijanmasu*

:512\13\23:upadraṣṭānumantā ca bhartā bhoktā maheśvaraḥ,
paramātmeti cāpyukto dehe'sminpuruṣaḥ paraḥ*

:513\13\24:ya evaṃ vetti puruṣaṃ prakṛtiṃ ca guṇaiḥ saha,
sarvathā vartamāno'pi na sa bhūyo'bhijāyate*

:514\13\25:dhyānenātmani paśyanti kecidātmānamātmanā,
anye sāṅkhyena yogena karmayogena cāpare*

:515\13\26:anye tvevamajānantaḥ śrutvānyebhya upāsate,
te'pi cātitarantyeva mṛtyuṃ śrutiparāyaṇāḥ*

:516\13\27:yāvatsañjāyate kiñcitsattvaṃ sthāvarajaṅgamam,
kṣetrakṣetrajñasaṃyogāttadviddhi bharatarṣabha*

:517\13\28:samaṃ sarveṣu bhūteṣu tiṣṭhantaṃ parameśvaram,
vinaśyatsvavinaśyantaṃ yaḥ paśyati sa paśyati*

:518\13\29:samaṃ paśyanhi sarvatra samavasthitamīśvaram,
na hinastyātmanātmānaṃ tato yāti parāṃ gatim*

:519\13\30:prakṛtyaiva ca karmāṇi kriyamāṇāni sarvaśaḥ,
yaḥ paśyati tathātmānamakartāraṃ sa paśyati*

:520\13\31:yadā bhūtapṛthagbhāvamekasthamanupaśyati,
tata eva ca vistāraṃ brahma sampadyate tadā*

:521\13\32:anāditvānnirguṇatvātparamātmāyamavyayaḥ,
śarīrastho'pi kaunteya na karoti na lipyate*

:522\13\33:yathā sarvagataṃ saukṣmyādākāśaṃ nopalipyate,
sarvatrāvasthito dehe tathātmā nopalipyate*

:523\13\34:yathā prakāśayatyekaḥ kṛtsnaṃ lokamimaṃ raviḥ,
kṣetraṃ kṣetrī tathā kṛtsnaṃ prakāśayati bhārata*

:524\13\35:kṣetrakṣetrajñayorevamantaraṃ jñānacakṣuṣā,
bhūtaprakṛtimokṣaṃ ca ye viduryānti te param*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde,
kṣetrakṣetrajñavibhāgayogo nāma trayodaśo'dhyāyaḥ*0*:13:*0*

:14:*Guṇatrayavibhāgayogaḥ*

atha caturdaśo'dhyāyaḥ, guṇatrayavibhāgayogaḥ

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:525\14\1:paraṃ bhūyaḥ pravakṣyāmi jñānānāṃ jñānamuttamam,
yajjñātvā munayaḥ sarve parāṃ siddhimito gatāḥ*

:526\14\2:idaṃ jñānamupāśritya mama sādharmyamāgatāḥ,
sarge'pi nopajāyante pralaye na vyathanti ca*

:527\14\3:mama yonirmahad brahma tasmingarbhaṃ dadhāmyaham,
sambhavaḥ sarvabhūtānāṃ tato bhavati bhārata*

:528\14\4:sarvayoniṣu kaunteya mūrtayaḥ sambhavanti yāḥ,
tāsāṃ brahma mahadyonirahaṃ bījapradaḥ pitā*

:529\14\5:sattvaṃ rajastama iti guṇāḥ prakṛtisambhavāḥ,
nibadhnanti mahābāho dehe dehinamavyayam*

:530\14\6:tatra sattvaṃ nirmalatvātprakāśakamanāmayam,
sukhasaṅgena badhnāti jñānasaṅgena cānagha*

:531\14\7:rajo rāgātmakaṃ viddhi tṛṣṇāsaṅgasamudbhavam,
tannibadhnāti kaunteya karmasaṅgena dehinam*

:532\14\8:tamastvajñānajaṃ viddhi mohanaṃ sarvadehinām,
pramādālasyanidrābhistannibadhnāti bhārata*

:533\14\9:sattvaṃ sukhe sañjayati rajaḥ karmaṇi bhārata,
jñānamāvṛtya tu tamaḥ pramāde sañjayatyuta*

:534\14\10:rajastamaścābhibhūya sattvaṃ bhavati bhārata,
rajaḥ sattvaṃ tamaścaiva tamaḥ sattvaṃ rajastathā*

:535\14\11:sarvadvāreṣu dehe'sminprakāśa upajāyate,
jñānaṃ yadā tadā vidyādvivṛddhaṃ sattvamityuta*

:536\14\12:lobhaḥ pravṛttirārambhaḥ karmaṇāmaśamaḥ spṛhā,
rajasyetāni jāyante vivṛddhe bharatarṣabha*

:537\14\13:aprakāśo'pravṛttiśca pramādo moha eva ca,
tamasyetāni jāyante vivṛddhe kurunandana*

:538\14\14:yadā sattve pravṛddhe tu pralayaṃ yāti dehabhṛt,
tadottamavidāṃ lokānamalānpratipadyate*

:539\14\15:rajasi pralayaṃ gatvā karmasaṅgiṣu jāyate,
tathā pralīnastamasi mūḍhayoniṣu jāyate*

:540\14\16:karmaṇaḥ sukṛtasyāhuḥ sāttvikaṃ nirmalaṃ phalam,
rajasastu phalaṃ duḥkhamajñānaṃ tamasaḥ phalam*

:541\14\17:sattvātsañjāyate jñānaṃ rajaso lobha eva ca,
pramādamohau tamaso bhavato'jñānameva ca*

:542\14\18:ūrdhvaṃ gacchanti sattvasthā madhye tiṣṭhanti rājasāḥ,
jaghanyaguṇavṛttisthā adho gacchanti tāmasāḥ*

:543\14\19:nānyaṃ guṇebhyaḥ kartāraṃ yadā draṣṭānupaśyati,
guṇebhyaśca paraṃ vetti madbhāvaṃ so'dhigacchati*

:544\14\20:guṇānetānatītya trīndehī dehasamudbhavān,
janmamṛtyujarāduḥkhairvimukto'mṛtamaśnute*

arjuna uvāca,

:545\14\21:kairliṅgaistrīnguṇānetānatīto bhavati prabho,
kimācāraḥ kathaṃ caitāṃstrīnguṇānativartate*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:546\14\22:prakāśaṃ ca pravṛttiṃ ca mohameva ca pāṇḍava,
na dveṣṭi sampravṛttāni na nivṛttāni kāṅkṣati*

:547\14\23:udāsīnavadāsīno guṇairyo na vicālyate,
guṇā vartanta ityevaṃ yo'vatiṣṭhati neṅgate*

:548\14\24:samaduḥkhasukhaḥ svasthaḥ samaloṣṭāśmakāñcanaḥ,
tulyapriyāpriyo dhīrastulyanindātmasaṃstutiḥ*

:549\14\25:mānāpamānayostulyastulyo mitrāripakṣayoḥ,
sarvārambhaparityāgī guṇātītaḥ sa ucyate*

:550\14\26:māṃ ca yo'vyabhicāreṇa bhaktiyogena sevate,
sa guṇānsamatītyaitānbrahmabhūyāya kalpate*

:551\14\27:brahmaṇo hi pratiṣṭhāhamamṛtasyāvyayasya ca,
śāśvatasya ca dharmasya sukhasyaikāntikasya ca*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde,
guṇatrayavibhāgayogo nāma caturdaśo'dhyāyaḥ*0*:14:*0*

:15:*Puruṣottamayogaḥ*

atha pañcadaśo'dhyāyaḥ, puruṣottamayogaḥ

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:552\15\1:ūrdhvamūlamadhaḥśākhamaśvatthaṃ prāhuravyayam,
chandāṃsi yasya parṇāni yastaṃ veda sa vedavit*

:553\15\2:adhaścordhvaṃ prasṛtāstasya śākhā guṇapravṛddhā viṣayapravālāḥ,
adhaśca mūlānyanusantatāni karmānubandhīni manuṣyaloke*

:554\15\3:na rūpamasyeha tathopalabhyate nānto na cādirna ca sampratiṣṭhā,
aśvatthamenaṃ suvirūḍhamūlaṃ asaṅgaśastreṇa dṛḍhena chittvā*

:555\15\4:tataḥ padaṃ tatparimārgitavyaṃ yasmingatā na nivartanti bhūyaḥ,
tameva cādyaṃ puruṣaṃ prapadye yataḥ pravṛttiḥ prasṛtā purāṇī*

:556\15\5:nirmānamohā jitasaṅgadoṣā adhyātmanityā vinivṛttakāmāḥ,
dvandvairvimuktāḥ sukhaduḥkhasaṃjñair gacchantyamūḍhāḥ padamavyayaṃ tat*

:557\15\6:na tadbhāsayate sūryo na śaśāṅko na pāvakaḥ,

:558\15\7:mamaivāṃśo jīvaloke jīvabhūtaḥ sanātanaḥ,

:559\15\8:śarīraṃ yadavāpnoti yaccāpyutkrāmatīśvaraḥ,

:560\15\9:śrotraṃ cakṣuḥ sparśanaṃ ca rasanaṃ ghrāṇameva ca,

:561\15\10:utkrāmantaṃ sthitaṃ vāpi bhuñjānaṃ vā guṇānvitam,
vimūḍhā nānupaśyanti paśyanti jñānacakṣuṣaḥ*

:562\15\11:yatanto yoginaścainaṃ paśyantyātmanyavasthitam,
yatanto'pyakṛtātmāno nainaṃ paśyantyacetasaḥ*

:563\15\12:yadādityagataṃ tejo jagadbhāsayate'khilam,
yaccandramasi yaccāgnau tattejo viddhi māmakam*

:564\15\13:gāmāviśya ca bhūtāni dhārayāmyahamojasā,
puṣṇāmi cauṣadhīḥ sarvāḥ somo bhūtvā rasātmakaḥ*

:565\15\14:ahaṃ vaiśvānaro bhūtvā prāṇināṃ dehamāśritaḥ,
prāṇāpānasamāyuktaḥ pacāmyannaṃ caturvidham*

sarvasya cāhaṃ hṛdi sanniviṣṭo mattaḥ smṛtirjñānamapohanañca,

:567\15\16:dvāvimau puruṣau loke kṣaraścākṣara eva ca,

:568\15\17:uttamaḥ puruṣastvanyaḥ paramātmetyudāhṛtaḥ,

:569\15\18:yasmātkṣaramatīto'hamakṣarādapi cottamaḥ,

:570\15\19:yo māmevamasammūḍho jānāti puruṣottamam,

:571\15\20:iti guhyatamaṃ śāstramidamuktaṃ mayānagha,
etadbuddhvā buddhimānsyātkṛtakṛtyaśca bhārata*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,

puruṣottamayogo nāma pañcadaśo'dhyāyaḥ*0*:15:*0*

:16:*Daivāsurasampadvibhāgayogaḥ*

atha ṣoḍaśo'dhyāyaḥ, daivāsurasampadvibhāgayogaḥ

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:572\16\1:abhayaṃ sattvasaṃśuddhirjñānayogavyavasthitiḥ,
dānaṃ damaśca yajñaśca svādhyāyastapa ārjavam*

:573\16\2:ahiṃsā satyamakrodhastyāgaḥ śāntirapaiśunam,

:574\16\3:tejaḥ kṣamā dhṛtiḥ śaucamadroho nātimānitā,

:575\16\4:dambho darpo'bhimānaśca krodhaḥ pāruṣyameva ca,
ajñānaṃ cābhijātasya pārtha sampadamāsurīm*

:576\16\5:daivī sampadvimokṣāya nibandhāyāsurī matā,

:577\16\6:dvau bhūtasargau loke'smindaiva āsura eva ca,
daivo vistaraśaḥ prokta āsuraṃ pārtha me śṛṇu*

:578\16\7:pravṛttiṃ ca nivṛttiṃ ca janā na vidurāsurāḥ,
na śaucaṃ nāpi cācāro na satyaṃ teṣu vidyate*

:579\16\8:asatyamapratiṣṭhaṃ te jagadāhuranīśvaram,
aparasparasambhūtaṃ kimanyatkāmahaitukam*

:580\16\9:etāṃ dṛṣṭimavaṣṭabhya naṣṭātmāno'lpabuddhayaḥ,
prabhavantyugrakarmāṇaḥ kṣayāya jagato'hitāḥ*

:581\16\10:kāmamāśritya duṣpūraṃ dambhamānamadānvitāḥ,

:582\16\11:cintāmaparimeyāṃ ca pralayāntāmupāśritāḥ,
kāmopabhogaparamā etāvaditi niścitāḥ*

:583\16\12:āśāpāśaśatairbaddhāḥ kāmakrodhaparāyaṇāḥ,
īhante kāmabhogārthamanyāyenārthasañcayān*

:584\16\13:idamadya mayā labdhamimaṃ prāpsye manoratham,
idamastīdamapi me bhaviṣyati punardhanam*

:585\16\14:asau mayā hataḥ śatrurhaniṣye cāparānapi,
īśvaro'hamahaṃ bhogī siddho'haṃ balavānsukhī*

:586\16\15:āḍhyo'bhijanavānasmi ko'nyo'sti sadṛśo mayā,
yakṣye dāsyāmi modiṣya ityajñānavimohitāḥ*

:587\16\16:anekacittavibhrāntā mohajālasamāvṛtāḥ,
prasaktāḥ kāmabhogeṣu patanti narake'śucau*

:588\16\17:ātmasambhāvitāḥ stabdhā dhanamānamadānvitāḥ,
yajante nāmayajñaiste dambhenāvidhipūrvakam*

:589\16\18:ahaṅkāraṃ balaṃ darpaṃ kāmaṃ krodhaṃ ca saṃśritāḥ,
māmātmaparadeheṣu pradviṣanto'bhyasūyakāḥ*

:590\16\19:tānahaṃ dviṣataḥ krūrānsaṃsāreṣu narādhamān,
kṣipāmyajasramaśubhānāsurīṣveva yoniṣu*

:591\16\20:āsurīṃ yonimāpannā mūḍhā janmani janmani,
māmaprāpyaiva kaunteya tato yāntyadhamāṃ gatim*

:592\16\21:trividhaṃ narakasyedaṃ dvāraṃ nāśanamātmanaḥ,
kāmaḥ krodhastathā lobhastasmādetattrayaṃ tyajet*

:593\16\22:etairvimuktaḥ kaunteya tamodvāraistribhirnaraḥ,
ācaratyātmanaḥ śreyastato yāti parāṃ gatim*

:594\16\23:yaḥ śāstravidhimutsṛjya vartate kāmakārataḥ,
na sa siddhimavāpnoti na sukhaṃ na parāṃ gatim*

:595\16\24:tasmācchāstraṃ pramāṇaṃ te kāryākāryavyavasthitau,
jñātvā śāstravidhānoktaṃ karma kartumihārhasi*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde,
daivāsurasampadvibhāgayogo nāma ṣoḍaśo'dhyāyaḥ*0*:16:*0*

:17:*Śraddhātrayavibhāgayogaḥ*

atha saptadaśo'dhyāyaḥ, śraddhātrayavibhāgayogaḥ

arjuna uvāca,

:596\17\1:ye śāstravidhimutsṛjya yajante śraddhayānvitāḥ,
teṣāṃ niṣṭhā tu kā kṛṣṇa sattvamāho rajastamaḥ*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:597\17\2:trividhā bhavati śraddhā dehināṃ sā svabhāvajā,
sāttvikī rājasī caiva tāmasī ceti tāṃ śṛṇu*

:598\17\3:sattvānurūpā sarvasya śraddhā bhavati bhārata,
śraddhāmayo'yaṃ puruṣo yo yacchraddhaḥ sa eva saḥ*

:599\17\4:yajante sāttvikā devānyakṣarakṣāṃsi rājasāḥ,
pretānbhūtagaṇāṃścānye yajante tāmasā janāḥ*

:600\17\5:aśāstravihitaṃ ghoraṃ tapyante ye tapo janāḥ,
dambhāhaṅkārasaṃyuktāḥ kāmarāgabalānvitāḥ*

:601\17\6:karṣayantaḥ śarīrasthaṃ bhūtagrāmamacetasaḥ,
māṃ caivāntaḥśarīrasthaṃ tānviddhyāsuraniścayān*

:602\17\7:āhārastvapi sarvasya trividho bhavati priyaḥ,
yajñastapastathā dānaṃ teṣāṃ bhedamimaṃ śṛṇu*

:603\17\8:āyuḥsattvabalārogyasukhaprītivivardhanāḥ,
rasyāḥ snigdhāḥ sthirā hṛdyā āhārāḥ sāttvikapriyāḥ*

:604\17\9:kaṭvamlalavaṇātyuṣṇatīkṣṇarūkṣavidāhinaḥ,
āhārā rājasasyeṣṭā duḥkhaśokāmayapradāḥ*

:605\17\10:yātayāmaṃ gatarasaṃ pūti paryuṣitaṃ ca yat,
ucchiṣṭamapi cāmedhyaṃ bhojanaṃ tāmasapriyam*

:606\17\11:aphalākāṅkṣibhiryajño vidhidṛṣṭo ya ijyate,
yaṣṭavyameveti manaḥ samādhāya sa sāttvikaḥ*

:607\17\12:abhisandhāya tu phalaṃ dambhārthamapi caiva yat,
ijyate bharataśreṣṭha taṃ yajñaṃ viddhi rājasam*

:608\17\13:vidhihīnamasṛṣṭānnaṃ mantrahīnamadakṣiṇam,
śraddhāvirahitaṃ yajñaṃ tāmasaṃ paricakṣate*

:609\17\14:devadvijaguruprājñapūjanaṃ śaucamārjavam,
brahmacaryamahiṃsā ca śārīraṃ tapa ucyate*

:610\17\15:anudvegakaraṃ vākyaṃ satyaṃ priyahitaṃ ca yat,
svādhyāyābhyasanaṃ caiva vāṅmayaṃ tapa ucyate*

:611\17\16:manaḥ prasādaḥ saumyatvaṃ maunamātmavinigrahaḥ,
bhāvasaṃśuddhirityetattapo mānasamucyate*

:612\17\17:śraddhayā parayā taptaṃ tapastattrividhaṃ naraiḥ,
aphalākāṅkṣibhiryuktaiḥ sāttvikaṃ paricakṣate*

:613\17\18:satkāramānapūjārthaṃ tapo dambhena caiva yat,
kriyate tadiha proktaṃ rājasaṃ calamadhruvam*

:614\17\19:mūḍhagrāheṇātmano yatpīḍayā kriyate tapaḥ,
parasyotsādanārthaṃ vā tattāmasamudāhṛtam*

:615\17\20:dātavyamiti yaddānaṃ dīyate'nupakāriṇe,
deśe kāle ca pātre ca taddānaṃ sāttvikaṃ smṛtam*

:616\17\21:yattu pratyupakārārthaṃ phalamuddiśya vā punaḥ,
dīyate ca parikliṣṭaṃ taddānaṃ rājasaṃ smṛtam*

:617\17\22:adeśakāle yaddānamapātrebhyaśca dīyate,
asatkṛtamavajñātaṃ tattāmasamudāhṛtam*

:618\17\23:Om̃tatsaditi nirdeśo brahmaṇastrividhaḥ smṛtaḥ,
brāhmaṇāstena vedāśca yajñāśca vihitāḥ purā*

:619\17\24:tasmādomityudāhṛtya yajñadānatapaḥkriyāḥ,

:620\17\25:tadityanabhisandhāya phalaṃ yajñatapaḥkriyāḥ,

:621\17\26:sadbhāve sādhubhāve ca sadityetatprayujyate,

:622\17\27:yajñe tapasi dāne ca sthitiḥ saditi cocyate,

:623\17\28:aśraddhayā hutaṃ dattaṃ tapastaptaṃ kṛtaṃ ca yat,
asadityucyate pārtha na ca tatpretya no iha*

Om̃ tatsaditi śrīmadbhagavadgītāsūpaniṣatsu,
brahmavidyāyāṃ yogaśāstre śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde,

:18:*Mokṣasaṃnyāsayogaḥ*

athāṣṭādaśo'dhyāyaḥ, mokṣasaṃnyāsayogaḥ

arjuna uvāca,

:624\18\1:saṃnyāsasya mahābāho tattvamicchāmi veditum,
tyāgasya ca hṛṣīkeśa pṛthakkeśiniṣūdana*

śrībhagavānuvāca,

:625\18\2:kāmyānāṃ karmaṇāṃ nyāsaṃ saṃnyāsaṃ kavayo viduḥ,
sarvakarmaphalatyāgaṃ prāhustyāgaṃ vicakṣaṇāḥ*

:626\18\3:tyājyaṃ doṣavadityeke karma prāhurmanīṣiṇaḥ,
yajñadānatapaḥkarma na tyājyamiti cāpare*

:627\18\4:niścayaṃ śṛṇu me tatra tyāge bharatasattama,
tyāgo hi puruṣavyāghra trividhaḥ samprakīrtitaḥ*

:628\18\5:yajñadānatapaḥkarma na tyājyaṃ kāryameva tat,
yajño dānaṃ tapaścaiva pāvanāni manīṣiṇām*

:629\18\6:etānyapi tu karmāṇi saṅgaṃ tyaktvā phalāni ca,
kartavyānīti me pārtha niścitaṃ matamuttamam*

:630\18\7:niyatasya tu saṃnyāsaḥ karmaṇo nopapadyate,
mohāttasya parityāgastāmasaḥ parikīrtitaḥ*

:631\18\8:duḥkhamityeva yatkarma kāyakleśabhayāttyajet,
sa kṛtvā rājasaṃ tyāgaṃ naiva tyāgaphalaṃ labhet*

:632\18\9:kāryamityeva yatkarma niyataṃ kriyate'rjuna,
saṅgaṃ tyaktvā phalaṃ caiva sa tyāgaḥ sāttviko mataḥ*

:633\18\10:na dveṣṭyakuśalaṃ karma kuśale nānuṣajjate,
tyāgī sattvasamāviṣṭo medhāvī chinnasaṃśayaḥ*

:634\18\11:na hi dehabhṛtā śakyaṃ tyaktuṃ karmāṇyaśeṣataḥ,
yastu karmaphalatyāgī sa tyāgītyabhidhīyate*

:635\18\12:aniṣṭamiṣṭaṃ miśraṃ ca trividhaṃ karmaṇaḥ phalam,
bhavatyatyāgināṃ pretya na tu saṃnyāsināṃ kvacit*

:636\18\13:pañcaitāni mahābāho kāraṇāni nibodha me,
sāṅkhye kṛtānte proktāni siddhaye sarvakarmaṇām*

:637\18\14:adhiṣṭhānaṃ tathā kartā karaṇaṃ ca pṛthagvidham,
vividhāśca pṛthakceṣṭā daivaṃ caivātra pañcamam*

:638\18\15:śarīravāṅmanobhiryatkarma prārabhate naraḥ,
nyāyyaṃ vā viparītaṃ vā pañcaite tasya hetavaḥ*

:639\18\16:tatraivaṃ sati kartāramātmānaṃ kevalaṃ tu yaḥ,
paśyatyakṛtabuddhitvānna sa paśyati durmatiḥ*

:640\18\17:yasya nāhaṅkṛto bhāvo buddhiryasya na lipyate,
hatvā'pi sa imā~llokānna hanti na nibadhyate*

:641\18\18:jñānaṃ jñeyaṃ parijñātā trividhā karmacodanā,
karaṇaṃ karma karteti trividhaḥ karmasaṅgrahaḥ*

:642\18\19:jñānaṃ karma ca kartā ca tridhaiva guṇabhedataḥ,
procyate guṇasaṅkhyāne yathāvacchṛṇu tānyapi*

:643\18\20:sarvabhūteṣu yenaikaṃ bhāvamavyayamīkṣate,
avibhaktaṃ vibhakteṣu tajjñānaṃ viddhi sāttvikam*

:644\18\21:pṛthaktvena tu yajjñānaṃ nānābhāvānpṛthagvidhān,
vetti sarveṣu bhūteṣu tajjñānaṃ viddhi rājasam*

:645\18\22:yattu kṛtsnavadekasminkārye saktamahaitukam,
atattvārthavadalpaṃ ca tattāmasamudāhṛtam*

:646\18\23:niyataṃ saṅgarahitamarāgadveṣataḥ kṛtam,
aphalaprepsunā karma yattatsāttvikamucyate*

:647\18\24:yattu kāmepsunā karma sāhaṅkāreṇa vā punaḥ,
kriyate bahulāyāsaṃ tadrājasamudāhṛtam*

:648\18\25:anubandhaṃ kṣayaṃ hiṃsāmanapekṣya ca pauruṣam,
mohādārabhyate karma yattattāmasamucyate*

:649\18\26:muktasaṅgo'nahaṃvādī dhṛtyutsāhasamanvitaḥ,
siddhyasiddhyornirvikāraḥ kartā sāttvika ucyate*

:650\18\27:rāgī karmaphalaprepsurlubdho hiṃsātmako'śuciḥ,
harṣaśokānvitaḥ kartā rājasaḥ parikīrtitaḥ*

:651\18\28:ayuktaḥ prākṛtaḥ stabdhaḥ śaṭho naiṣkṛtiko'lasaḥ,
viṣādī dīrghasūtrī ca kartā tāmasa ucyate*

:652\18\29:buddherbhedaṃ dhṛteścaiva guṇatastrividhaṃ śṛṇu,
procyamānamaśeṣeṇa pṛthaktvena dhanañjaya*

:653\18\30:pravṛttiṃ ca nivṛttiṃ ca kāryākārye bhayābhaye,
bandhaṃ mokṣaṃ ca yā vetti buddhiḥ sā pārtha sāttvikī*

:654\18\31:yayā dharmamadharmaṃ ca kāryaṃ cākāryameva ca,
ayathāvatprajānāti buddhiḥ sā pārtha rājasī*

:655\18\32:adharmaṃ dharmamiti yā manyate tamasāvṛtā,
sarvārthānviparītāṃśca buddhiḥ sā pārtha tāmasī*

:656\18\33:dhṛtyā yayā dhārayate manaḥprāṇendriyakriyāḥ,
yogenāvyabhicāriṇyā dhṛtiḥ sā pārtha sāttvikī*

:657\18\34:yayā tu dharmakāmārthāndhṛtyā dhārayate'rjuna,
prasaṅgena phalākāṅkṣī dhṛtiḥ sā pārtha rājasī*

:658\18\35:yayā svapnaṃ bhayaṃ śokaṃ viṣādaṃ madameva ca,
na vimuñcati durmedhā dhṛtiḥ sā pārtha tāmasī*

:659\18\36:sukhaṃ tvidānīṃ trividhaṃ śṛṇu me bharatarṣabha,
abhyāsādramate yatra duḥkhāntaṃ ca nigacchati*

:660\18\37:yattadagre viṣamiva pariṇāme'mṛtopamam,
tatsukhaṃ sāttvikaṃ proktamātmabuddhiprasādajam*

:661\18\38:viṣayendriyasaṃyogādyattadagre'mṛtopamam,
pariṇāme viṣamiva tatsukhaṃ rājasaṃ smṛtam*

:662\18\39:yadagre cānubandhe ca sukhaṃ mohanamātmanaḥ,
nidrālasyapramādotthaṃ tattāmasamudāhṛtam*

:663\18\40:na tadasti pṛthivyāṃ vā divi deveṣu vā punaḥ,
sattvaṃ prakṛtijairmuktaṃ yadebhiḥ syāttribhirguṇaiḥ*

:664\18\41:brāhmaṇakṣatriyaviśāṃ śūdrāṇāṃ ca parantapa,
karmāṇi pravibhaktāni svabhāvaprabhavairguṇaiḥ*

:665\18\42:śamo damastapaḥ śaucaṃ kṣāntirārjavameva ca,
jñānaṃ vijñānamāstikyaṃ brahmakarma svabhāvajam*

:666\18\43:śauryaṃ tejo dhṛtirdākṣyaṃ yuddhe cāpyapalāyanam,
dānamīśvarabhāvaśca kṣātraṃ karma svabhāvajam*

:667\18\44:kṛṣigaurakṣyavāṇijyaṃ vaiśyakarma svabhāvajam,
paricaryātmakaṃ karma śūdrasyāpi svabhāvajam*

:668\18\45:sve sve karmaṇyabhirataḥ saṃsiddhiṃ labhate naraḥ,
svakarmanirataḥ siddhiṃ yathā vindati tacchṛṇu*

:669\18\46:yataḥ pravṛttirbhūtānāṃ yena sarvamidaṃ tatam,
svakarmaṇā tamabhyarcya siddhiṃ vindati mānavaḥ*

:670\18\47:śreyānsvadharmo viguṇaḥ paradharmātsvanuṣṭhitāt,
svabhāvaniyataṃ karma kurvannāpnoti kilbiṣam*

:671\18\48:sahajaṃ karma kaunteya sadoṣamapi na tyajet,
sarvārambhā hi doṣeṇa dhūmenāgnirivāvṛtāḥ*

:672\18\49:asaktabuddhiḥ sarvatra jitātmā vigataspṛhaḥ,
