1/1/1.
saŋ đīrâmrih ayudđa riŋ raṇa tapabrata makalaga wīraṣaḍripu,
lagyabyūha samāđitattwaguṇa wāhananira karuṇādiđāraṇā,
sanmudrā đwaja siṅhanāda japamantra warađanuh acintyaƀāwanā,
bođijñāna śarottamâṅhilaṅakĕn ripu makaꝑala đaṙmmaśūnyatā*

1/2/2.
sākṣāt mūṙtti ƀaṭāra śākyamuni mārawijaya sira saŋ samaṅkana,
maṅgĕḥ maṅgalaniŋ maṅö praṇata ƀaktyaśiꝁara ri lĕbūni jöŋ nira,
stutyaṅkĕn pratimāpratiṣṫa ginĕlaṙ makuṭawiđi winījan akṣara,
hinyaŋ ni ṅwaṅ amoǥa tan saha sake padanira mara janma ni ṅhulun*

1/3/3.
lāwan deniṅ aṅaṙccanê pada ƀaṭāra sugata pinakeṣṭidewatā,
niṙwiǥnā ṅwaṅ amaṙṇna-maṙṇna mikĕtaŋ caritaniran ađaṙmmadeśanā,
Aṅkĕn cāmaniyâmrakāśakĕna kastawanira pinawitra riŋ jagat,
paṅgil rakwa wĕnaŋ paṅahwata ri janmaniṅ ajar-ajaraŋrĕgĕp laṅö*

1/4/4.
ndaḥ sambođanani ṅhulun ri sira saŋ kawi nipuṇa pinaṇḍitêŋ laṅö,
ndātan kojara talpakâṅidanidan ṅwaṅ aṅikĕta kaŧā palambaṅa,
tan saṅkeŋ wihikan tuhun maṅiriṅêŋ gati para kawi mātra riŋ laṅö,
tan samwas pwa kuraŋ paṅawruh apa tan kawi kumawi turuŋ wruh iŋ krama*

1/5/5.
sotan ta ṅwaṅ apuṅguṅ atpada maṅö pati-tut i-saṅ akīṙtti ƀāṣita,
tonĕn lwiṙ nya hirim-hirim sama saroruha riṅ apa ya sāđya tuñjuṅa,
maṅkā teki hatuṙ nya tan sasiriṅêŋ para kawi sapaniwyan iŋ lanö,
Aṅhiŋ teki Inantusâriwariwāṅikĕta carita de kawīśwara*

1/6/6.
wwantĕn kuñjarakaṙṇna teki paṅaranya carita ya ta dadya pādika,
ndan wyaṙŧênapus iŋ kadi ṅwaṅ aḍusun wiꝑala kadi mahâpupuṙ harĕŋ,
simbantĕn makahaywaniŋ carita maṅkin amuwuhi panindaniŋ para,
wyaktinyâmuhareki hāsyakaraṇa ṅhiṅ apuran iki de mahājana*

1/7/7.
śrī wꜽrocana pūṙwwa niŋ carita kālanira pinarĕk iŋ watĕk sura,
ṅkānên bođiwihāra niṙmmala samaṅkana ta siran ađaṙmmadeśanā,
Akṣoƀyādi sabođisattwapariwāra marĕki sira ƀaktyamūṙṣita,
mwaŋ sakweḥ para bajrapāṇisuranāŧa yama baruṇa len đanāđipa#

2/1/8.
ƀarālipratisāra labđê samaya,
suđaṙmmāđikatattwa sāmpun katama,
wĕkasniŋ paramāṙŧikôwus kahiḍĕp,
waraḥ śrī ṣaḍaƀijña kāruṇyamaya*

2/2/9.
tĕlas rakwa ƀarālyamūjā praŧama,
gumantyaŋ hyaṅ anukramâyajña marĕk,
sawaŋ wrĕṣṭyamrĕtaŋ suđaṙmmaśrawaṇa,
Awas lwiṙ trĕṇa budđiniŋ hyaŋ masawuŋ*

2/3/10.
huwus pwêki dineśanan đaṙmma kabeḥ,
tĕkap hyaŋ jina tuṣṭa haṙṣan rumĕṅö,
paḍâṅaṙccana sādare śrī sugata,
byatītan ry-ulih iŋ watĕk hyaŋ sakala#

3/1/11.
Aŧa kuñjarakaṙṇna carita matapêŋ waramerugiri,
pinakāgratapođara tan alupa riŋ sugatasmaraṇa,
makasāđana yoganira saꝑalawīṙyya sināđyanira,
tuwi rākṣasarūpa pinakamala hetuniran patapa*

3/2/12.
hana teki ri lambuh iṅ acala gihāśrama pūṙwwamuꝁa,
Aṅuṅaŋ jalađi prasama kadi tapowana riŋ gagana,
sĕh ikaŋ kusumâṅjrah aparimita saṙwwa ꝑalanya-n atöb,
panĕḍĕṅnika tan pĕgat asira-siran kadi tan lĕwasa*

3/3/13.
ri hujuŋ-hujuṅ iŋ paraṅ arĕja maṅuṅkul anoṅi juraŋ,
matĕḍun-tĕḍunan bañu kumusuh aǥoṣa humuŋ gumuruḥ,
Asurak lwir i-cāmaranika magiraṅgiraṅ ādrĕs aṅin,
Anaṅis mara cātakanika hana möṙ ƀramitêŋ gagana*

3/4/14.
wruh agöŋ prihikaŋ giriwana kayu teja samâśryasinaŋ,
tĕkaniŋ kayu simbarĕn aniru wiƀūṣyaṅĕne wwit ikā,
Akaluṅkaluṅ odwad araras agĕlaṅgĕlaṅ aṙjja kuniŋ,
hana wrĕkṣa kawuntat umarĕk irikaŋ walik-āḍĕp i-sor#

4/1/15.
sawaŋ strīratna lwiṙ niṅ acala lĕṅöŋ lĕŋlĕŋ alaṅö,
Asiñjaŋ ñjrahniŋ pādapa huwus aganđāsĕkar arūm,
ǥanāraŋ wastranyeki putih anawaŋ caṅlwi sahĕle,
sawitnyan heṙ-talyāƀaraṇanika tejākara-kara*

4/2/16.
rarasniŋ kambaŋ pahyas ikin awuwuḥ rūm hayu tinon,
baṅun līlā haṙṣan pasidĕha latā mĕṅgĕp aśila,
lĕnöŋ rūpanya dyaḥ lalita maṅilo toya walahar,
huyaŋ ganya-ŋ rāt kāraṇaniṅ apĕpĕt patra kasirir*

4/3/17.
hana lwiṙ śrĕṅgāra-ŋ gagana lawan adrîḍĕpanika,
gĕrĕḥ mandrâlon paŋharih-arih i-śabdanya karĕṅö,
kilat ramyâṅde raśmi paṅujiwatanyâmrih akire,
mĕluk mālap-soṙ waṅkawanika tĕkâñumbana bañu*

4/4/18.
katon mātra jro pambĕkan i-bañuniŋ paṙwwatanadī,
Arĕṅwâṅ-imba lwiṙ halis iṅ anukĕṙ wāhu kawawa,
kapiṅsĕl wwahniŋ nyuḥ gaḍiṅ apiṅit emweŋ jala papaḥ,
ri sahniŋ meǥāpiṇḍa mawudawudâwrĕkṣa katilar*

4/5/19.
Alūm aṅliḥ taḥ pādapa lĕsu katīkṣṇan dinakara,
tuṣārâṅluḥ hisnya ƀramara manaṅis möṙ kalĕmahan,
sawaḥ rāhniŋ siñjaŋ drawa mara tahi-hyaṅnika mabāŋ,
tumuṅkultuṅkul mraknika mapa tikaŋ wastu dinĕlö#

5/1/20.
tuhu mara yan wiśeṣagirirāja sakahawan aṅāśrayêŋ laṅö,
Upaśama tiṅkah iŋ tahĕn ataṅkil apagĕh apa tan salaḥ-ƀawa,
praṇata maṅañjali lwir i-suluṙ nya sahaja marĕk aŋharas lĕmaḥ,
parĕṅ ahatuṙ sĕkaṙ ƀujagapuṣpa suraƀi sahaśoka campaka*

5/2/21.
Arĕṇa Apawwahan gaḍiṅ ikaŋ tirisan amawa mañcuṅ aŋlugas,
jalađa ri Agraniŋ gĕgĕr aṅuṅkul amisah asawaŋ payuŋ putiḥ,
kahaḍaṅ asaṅgani swarani śaṅꝁanika humuṅ i-gĕṇḍiṅ iŋ manuk,
kadi hana bañwahoma titis iŋ juraṅ anĕkar-uraŋ sĕkaṙ tibā*

5/3/22.
wwara hawan iŋ wanāśrama tinuṇḍa pinarigi hinambalan watu,
sana-sini bañjaran sĕkar arūm sĕḍĕṅ amĕwĕh aṅambwakĕn laṅö,
cara-cara handwaṅ asla kayu mas kayu puriṅ acĕlĕkcĕlĕk putiḥ,
parajita maśridanta jagaśatru pinaḍapaḍa len sĕkaṙ kuniŋ*

5/4/23.
paḍa tan asoṙ sĕkaṙ niṅ asanâsinaṅ akuniṅ apendah iŋ kuniŋ,
wijah apajöŋ kuniŋ kunĕṅ ikaŋ kusuma mĕkar atöb paḍâkuniŋ,
tĕka ni manuk nikâkuniṅ ikaŋ kapudaṅ anakĕbinya waḥ kuniŋ,
tuhu lakuniŋ hanêŋ gunuṅ awaṙṇna kasih-arĕp agañjaran kuniŋ*

5/5/24.
patani pararyanan hana mahāntĕn apatiga lĕyĕp kadîŋ tulis,
hatur ika sopacāra taru saṙwwaꝑala kusuma rāmya len pucaŋ,
hujuṅan i-patraniŋ pisaṅ-alas baṅun aharĕpaṅ āptyaneŋ mara,
bañu tan adoḥ ri soṙ titir aṅeṙ-tali galita sakeŋ ruhuṙ wukir*

5/6/25.
kidaṅ aduluṙ laki stryasĕmu polah iṅ atawatawan marêŋ wukir,
liriṅ i-matanya lĕñjĕp amanis kadi maṅatag araryanêŋ priyā,
gaḍuṅ awilĕt siliḥ-pĕkul aluŋ lwir atuduh amarantya riŋ bukur,
baṅun amĕḍaṙ tĕṅaḥ pupus ikaŋ liraṅ aputih anaṅhi kūŋ lulut*

5/7/26.
Apan ika rakwa mandaragiri prakaśita pinakādiniŋ wukir,
satata saƀā watĕk hyaṅ awilāsa dinulur ikaṅ apsarâpsarī,
karaṇa niṅ adri rāmya racananya-n aniluman anopamêŋ laṅö,
Aƀinawa toya niṙmmala ri śrĕṅga hana raṇu patīṙŧan uttama*

5/8/27.
Apituwi śrĕṅganiŋ giri sumeru hibĕk atapa rĕṣyatūt gĕgĕr,
saḍukuḍukuḥ katon atĕp i-raṅkaṅ ika pĕtuṅ anekawaṙṇnana,
hana taṅ inantya kāptin akĕtĕb-kĕtĕb anatap inuttamêŋ hajöŋ,
hana kumukus piliḥ ra tapa ganya sumĕlaṅ akulub-kulub gaṅan*

5/9/28.
Ika ta halĕpnya soṙ tĕkap iṅ āśrama patapaniraŋ mahāsura,
wiwara śilāputiḥ kadi tutuknikaṅ acala sumeru kātara,
Arata wiśāla maṙsik akilā sꝑaṭika wĕṅi jugā yayâpaḍaŋ,
bañu tumĕḍun sake ruhur aṅuṅkuli wiwara tibā marêŋ juraŋ*

5/10/29.
yatiwara kuñjarāsura hanêŋ wiwara sĕḍĕṅ ayoga đāraka,
Aśila subadđa lagyaṅisapu-ŋ taṅan alih amitāƀalakṣaṇa,
pinahabĕnĕṙ śarīranira niścala-n umulat iṅ agra nāsikā,
mari ta-n asattwađātu kahiḍĕp wimala waluya jāti śūnyatā*

5/11/30.
krama nira rākṣasa ndan iki lakṣaṇanira tuhu ƀāwaniŋ wiku,
tĕhĕr akaluŋ gaṇitri magĕlaŋ guduha rahat iṅ ambarâtĕṅö,
paragi tĕṅö sinampĕtakĕn uttama satirun atĕṅwa riŋ manaḥ,
kadi makuṭâjaṭâhalap ulaḥ saṅ ataki-taki niṣparigraha*

5/12/31.
ri huwus irâsamāđi suꝁacitta larinira mijil sakeŋ giha,
ƀramita wulat nirêŋ jalađi rāmya ri pasisi tĕkêŋ tĕṅaḥ lĕyĕp,
wĕkasan awaṙṇna miśra lawan ambara lĕṅit ahirĕŋ katiṅhalan,
linawĕ-lawö nira-n kadi wĕkasniṅ ahurip ana nitya niṣꝑala*

5/13/32.
kṣaṇa humĕnĕŋ sirâsĕmu wiraṅrwaṅ anuluyi manaḥ panasbaran,
gumuṇita gupta riŋ twas awiweka satĕkapanikaŋ nayottama,
tan alawasaŋ prayojana tumĕmwa wiƀawa tuwi marya rākṣasa,
karaṇa nirâkire marĕk anĕmbaha ri pada ƀaṭāra gꜷtama#

6/1/33.
saṅ ugratapa śīǥra maṅkat ahawan wiyat niṙƀaya,
hana-n kadi ꝁagendra maŋlayaṅ iṅ agraniŋ paṙwwata,
swaƀāwanira yakṣa śakti maduluṙ praƀāwâdƀuta,
prahāra kumusuḥ tĕkâdrĕs amusus tahĕn riŋ wukir*

6/2/34.
lĕpas larini râṅidul tucapa taŋ sakātuṅkulan,
pradeśa mapatuṅga-tuṅgalan akāṇḍa-kāṇḍâlaṅö,
tĕkêŋ wukir alasnya kubwan arĕṇĕb gagâtūt hiriŋ,
pucaŋ tirisan aryan aṙṇnĕb atatā tahĕn wwa-wwahan*

6/3/35.
gĕgĕṙ paṅalusan hañaṙ tinarukâpramāṇêŋ juraŋ,
huwus tinapak iŋ wanāgni sawalĕṙ-walĕṙ nyan gĕsĕŋ,
dudug tĕka riṅ agra mākrĕp atumaŋ harĕŋnyâpupul,
śilā kumalaśâputiḥ rĕṅat areꝁa-reꝁā pĕṭa*

6/4/36.
Ikaŋ gunuṅ aroŋ katon hana I-soṙ giha lwiṙ gĕḍoŋ,
paraŋ nika sumoŋ sajoŋ kadi masoŋ winoṅwoŋ mara,
sirôbwan umulat paḍâmawa wajoŋ kajoṅkoŋ tibā,
Aboṅ umuni kagyat awri ginĕroŋ nikaŋ moṅ alon*

6/5/37.
Adödĕṅ aputiḥ wwayiŋ walahar embuh akweḥ gunuŋ,
sawaḥ sꝑaṭikamāṙgga śudđa lumarap bañunyâlaris,
tutug tĕka ri winđyapaṙwwata paḍâṅawetan lĕpas,
tumampuh i-gihānikaŋ jalađi ǥūṙṇnitâgĕnturan*

6/6/38.
rĕrĕb sinawawêŋ riris kilat aliwran aƀrâsinaŋ,
sateja dumilaḥ praƀāwanira kuñjarâwyomaga,
paḍânah-anaha-n mulat kaburĕṅaŋ tumĕṅhêŋ laṅit,
mapêndah iki liŋ nikaŋ wwaṅ apa tan hanā maṅkana*

6/7/39.
tiniṅhalan irâparö giri sumeru lambwaŋ kidul,
baṅun sira sinamƀramêṅ alas akon marâraryana,
humuŋ swara nikaŋ manuk wija-wijaḥ lwir aswāgata,
lutuŋ nika lumumpat agya susugun kadi kṣepa ya*

6/8/40.
ƀawiṣyati katon krĕtâƀinawa taŋ wihārâhalĕp,
Agopura suwaṙṇna ratna mapucak maṇīndrāṙjjuna,
ndatan pagati taŋ diwākara śaśāṅka keraŋ-Iraŋ,
tĕkap niṅ atiƀāswarânĕlĕhi bapra śudđâkila*

6/9/41.
wawaŋ sira tĕkêŋ wihāra warabođicittâmala,
tumuntĕn umarĕk krĕtāñjali ri pāduka hyaŋ jina,
wruh iŋ paramadewakaṙṣaṇa wiśeṣapūjastuti,
ya kāraṇa ƀaṭāra tuṣṭa tumuluymanantwê sira*

6/10/42.
wĕkaṅku tija ƀāgya saŋ tapa marĕk ri jöŋ ni ṅhulun,
ndya donta laki yan parêry-aku mapaŋ pinintânaku,
huwus wruh aku yan tasak bratatapanta ṅūnîŋ wukir,
Usön tĕpĕtakĕn juga prih ikanaŋ kayogīśwaran*

6/11/43.
na liŋ nira ƀaṭāra budđa sumahuṙ ta saŋ tāpasa,
ƀaṭāra huniṅan ṅhulun warahaniŋ suđaṙmmāđika,
maran saꝑalajanma marya makajāti yakṣākrĕti,
Apan pisaniṅun manĕmwakĕna wīṙyya dewapraƀu*

6/12/44.
muwaḥ mapa ta wastu sambulih i-janmaniŋ rāt kabeḥ,
nimitta nika yan hana-ŋ tumĕmu duḥꝁa lāwan suꝁa,
lĕwĕs mara ri tan paḍanya hana kasy-asiḥ mwaŋ sugiḥ,
pitowi hana pūṙṇnarūpa sawaneḥ manaṇḍaŋ mala*

6/13/45.
nahan pataña saŋ tapāsura ri jöŋ nira hyaŋ jina,
pradīpta rawitulya rakwa karuṇa-n ƀaṭāreŋ jagat,
ya kāraṇani budđi saŋ yati mĕkaṙ sawaŋ paṅkaja,
halĕpnya kahatuṙ marêŋ pada ƀaṭāra tuṣṭâmuwus*

6/14/46.
ya têku laki janma mūlya maṅaran pawitreŋ jagat,
ri denyan aharĕp ri đaṙmma tuhu sāđu labđêŋ hayu,
Apan sipi kĕtaŋ hawas wihikaneŋ rusitniŋ dadi,
Amĕŋ­pĕṅa ri kālaniŋ hurip aṅiṣṭya đaṙmmôttama*

6/15/47.
hana wruh i-kadibyan iŋ paramađaṙmma yan gĕgwana,
taŧāpi tiḍapucꞓa tan harĕp atakwanêŋ paṇḍita,
pijĕṙ jĕnĕk amukti wīṙyya kasuꝁan ginönnyêniwö,
mataŋnya larapanya wismrĕti jaḍêŋ suđaṙmmakrama*

6/16/48.
swaƀāwanikanaŋ lalĕṙ maharĕp iŋ kudis rāḥ kanin,
purīṣa mahahā śrĕgāla bawi gāgak āptîŋ dagiŋ,
tukaṙ wyasana kāptiniŋ wwaṅ atimūḋa mūṙkꝁâwĕrö,
suđaṙmma hita kahyun iŋ paramasāđu tan gĕŋ galak*

6/17/49.
hana wwaṅ awamāna liŋnya taya đaṙmmaniŋ dewatā,
Anāgatanikaŋ gawe hala-hayu ndya donyan tĕmu,
nihan sanu sadrĕbyaniŋ hyaṅ alapĕnku norânulaḥ,
pitowin ika drĕwyaniŋ wwaṅ aparan kawĕdyêriya*

6/18/50.
Ikaŋ wwaṅ awamāna maṅkana pĕgat hiḍĕpnyeŋ hati,
ndatan tuhu pĕgat kasaṅśayan ikâwĕrö kewala,
lĕhĕŋ gati nikaŋ tahĕn sapadi janma mattâđama,
Apan dadi lĕpas pratiṣṫanĕn i-mantra saŋ paṇḍita#

7/1/51.
Āpan duṙllaƀa taŋ dadi wwaṅ inusiṙ niŋ saṙwwaƀāwêŋ jagat,
yadyan mānuṣa duṙllaƀa-ŋ wwaṅ atĕmaḥ jalwâmiśeṣêŋ priyā,
yan jalwâtika duṙllaƀa-ŋ wwaṅ atĕmaḥ widwān suśīlâpagĕḥ,
yadyan paṇḍita duṙllaƀā wihikanêŋ widyākriyādyāgama*

7/2/52.
yadyan wruḥ-wruha riŋ kriyāgama tuhun saŋ wruḥ riṅ aṙṣârusit,
